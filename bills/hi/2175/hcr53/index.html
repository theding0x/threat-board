HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES 


H.C.R. NO. 


53 




THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2025 







STATE OF HAWAII 

























HOUSE CONCURRENT 

RESOLUTION 





Urging the City and County of Honolulu and developers to
coordinate their infrastructure plans and collaborate with stakeholders to
ensure adequate infrastructure be in place prior to building new housing
developments . 














WHEREAS, Hawaii�s state infrastructure received an overall D+
rating, according to a report from the American Society of Civil Engineers; and 



WHEREAS, for decades, the needs of Hawaii
infrastructure have been overlooked, used beyond their structural life span,
and struggle from a lack of systemic financing; and 



WHEREAS, the State was fortunate to receive
a $6.6 million federal grant from the U.S. Department of Housing and Urban
Development, however, this hardly covers the estimated $1.1 billion in unfunded
infrastructure needs; and 



WHEREAS, the City and County of Honolulu
has had a poor track record with prematurely awarding development contracts;
and 



WHEREAS, developers continue to race to
build new homes without the proper infrastructure in place or the foresight for
the communities' needs following new housing developments; and 



WHEREAS, West Oahu has been experiencing an
influx of development without the proper upgrades to infrastructure; and 



WHEREAS, the issues of inadequate
infrastructure have led to the delays of countless housing options to address
the State's fifty-thousand-unit housing shortage; and 



WHEREAS, the 2024 annual report of the
Hawaii Interagency Council for Transit-Oriented Development found key
recommendations such as coordinating State and county infrastructure plans and
revising county policies to enhance existing tools and improve market conditions;
and 



WHEREAS, this body finds that the State
cannot adequately address the housing demands that residents have until
overcoming infrastructural needs; now, therefore, 





BE IT
RESOLVED by the House of Representatives of the Thirty-third Legislature of the
State of Hawaii, Regular Session of 2025, the Senate concurring, that the City and County of Honolulu and developers are
urged to coordinate their infrastructure plans and collaborate with
stakeholders to ensure adequate infrastructure be in place prior to building
new housing developments ; and

RESOLVED that certified copies of this Concurrent
