SECTION 1. The
legislature finds that the State's policy of providing highly subsidized parking

congestion or greenhouse gas emissions, and contradicts state policies to
encourage alternative modes of transportation.

The legislature further finds that for many

controlled lot located at or near the employees' workplace through pre-tax

offered at a rate of approximately one-third of the public rate. For example, employee parking rates in the

modes reduce the number of cars driven in the State and parked in state lots.
