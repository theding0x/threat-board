RELATING TO PUBLIC HOUSING EVICTIONS . 





BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII: 










SECTION 1. Section
356D-64, Hawaii Revised Statutes, is amended by amending subsection (e) to read
as follows: 

" (e) Upon eviction, the household goods and
personal effects of the tenant against whom the order is entered, and those of
any persons using the premises incident to the tenant &#42892; s
holding, may be removed from the premises and stored by the authority. If the action is taken, the authority shall
have a lien on the property taken for the expenses incurred by the authority in
moving and storing the property, and the authority [ is authorized to ] may 
sell or otherwise dispose of the property if unclaimed after [ thirty ] fourteen 
days. " 

SECTION 2. 
Section 356D-94, Hawaii Revised Statutes, is amended by amending
subsection (e) to read as follows: 

" (e) Upon eviction, the household goods and
personal effects of the person against whom the order is entered, and those of
any persons using the premises incident to the person &#42892; s
holding, may be removed from the premises and stored by the authority. If the action is taken, the authority shall
have a lien on the property so taken for the expenses incurred by it in moving

SECTION 3. 
Statutory material to be repealed is bracketed and stricken. New statutory material is underscored. 

SECTION 4. 
This Act shall take effect on July 1, 2050. 















Report Title: 

HPHA; Public
Housing; Evictions; Tenants; Unclaimed Property 



Description: 

Reduces
the time the Hawaii Public Housing Authority is required to store unclaimed
personal effects of a tenant evicted from public housing from thirty days to
fourteen days. Effective 7/1/2050. (SD1)
