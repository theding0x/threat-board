BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII: 










SECTION 1. The
legislature finds that the Hawaii housing finance and development corporation
is tasked with developing and financing low- and moderate-income housing
projects and administering homeownership programs. The department of Hawaiian home lands has a
mandate to provide native Hawaiians a homesteading benefit through leases for
residential, agricultural, or pastoral purposes. As the Hawaii housing finance and development
corporation and the department of Hawaiian home lands have similar missions to
provide residents with affordable housing, amending the existing composition of
the Hawaii housing finance and development corporation board of directors will
ensure the alignment of housing priorities while ensuring continued benefits
for Hawaii's people. 

Accordingly, the purpose of this Act is to
add the chairperson of the Hawaiian homes commission to the board of directors
of the Hawaii housing finance and development corporation.
