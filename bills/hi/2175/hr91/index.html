HOUSE RESOLUTION 





REQUESTING THE
DEPARTMENT OF EDUCATION TO POST PUBLICLY ACCESSIBLE INFORMATION ON ITS WEBSITE
PERTAINING TO CHANGES TO GUN SAFETY AND STORAGE LAWS. 














WHEREAS, the
presence of unsecured or improperly stored firearms contributes to a heightened
risk of accidental injury, accidental death, and theft or loss of firearms; and 



WHEREAS,
unsecured or improperly stored firearms significantly increase the risk of
unintentional injuries and death among children; and 



WHEREAS,
numerous states, including Hawaii, have passed or are considering the passage
of secure storage laws to prevent unauthorized firearm access by children; and 



WHEREAS,
parents and members of households with firearms should be actively updated as
to any changes to gun safety requirements, including secure storage laws; now,
therefore, 



BE IT
RESOLVED by the House of Representatives of the Thirty-third Legislature of the
State of Hawaii, Regular Session of 2025, that the Department of Education is
requested to post publicly accessible information on its website pertaining to
changes to gun safety and storage laws to ensure that students, parents, and
educators have up-to-date resources on secure firearm storage requirements; and 



BE IT FURTHER
RESOLVED that certified copies of this Resolution be transmitted to the
Chairperson of the Board of Education and Superintendent of Education.
