C.D. 1 















A BILL FOR AN ACT 





RELATING TO KUPUNA HOUSING . 





BE IT
ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII: 










SECTION 1. The
legislature finds that the State's kupuna are at significant risk of
homelessness. As rental prices have
experienced dramatic increases statewide, more kupuna, who are on fixed
incomes, struggle to secure affordable housing. 
Presently, the state rent supplement program for kupuna provides support
for qualified kupuna who are homeless or at imminent risk of being homeless. However, this critical program will sunset in
2026, creating uncertainty for existing participants in the program. Therefore, extending the sunset date will
ensure continued access to critical rent supplement assistance. 

Accordingly, the purpose of this Act is to
extend the sunset date of the state rent supplement program for kupuna to
continue to accommodate the housing needs of qualified participants. 

SECTION 2. 
Act 98, Session Laws of Hawaii 2023, is amended by amending section 6 to
read as follows: 

" SECTION
6. This Act shall take effect on July 1,
2023, and shall be repealed on June 30, [ 2026. ] 2028. " 

SECTION 3. 
Statutory material to be repealed is bracketed and stricken. New statutory material is underscored. 

SECTION 4. 
This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
