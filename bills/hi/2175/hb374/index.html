BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII: 










SECTION 1 . Section 201H-31, Hawaii Revised Statutes, is
amended to read as follows: 

" [ [ ]&sect;201H-31[ ] ] Criteria. 
(a) In
administering this chapter and other laws of the State
applicable to the supplying of housing or the assistance in obtaining housing ,
the corporation shall give preference to those
applicants most in need of assistance in obtaining housing, in light of the
amount of moneys available for the various programs . In doing so, the corporation shall take into
consideration [ the ] : 

(1) The applicant's household income
and number of dependents; [ the ] 

(2) The age of the applicant; [ the ] 

(3) The physical disabilities of the
applicant or those living with the applicant; [ whether or not ] 

(4) Whether the present housing of
the applicant is below standard; [ whether or not ] 

(5) Whether the applicant's need for
housing has arisen by reason of displacement of the applicant by governmental
actions; 

(6) The proximity between the housing
location and the applicant's place of employment; 

(7) Whether the applicant is a state or
county employee; and [ other ] 

(8) Other factors as it may deem
pertinent. 

The
corporation may allow households with incomes up to twenty per cent greater
than the income on which the maximum sales price was based to be qualified to
purchase a unit. 

(b) The corporation shall: 

(1) Determine the order of preferences
as outlined in this section and rank all applicants accordingly; 

(2) Select applicants based on
application date within the pool of similarly ranked applicants; and 

(3) Validate the preference status of an
applicant before occupancy of an affordable unit. 

(c) The corporation may establish additional
eligibility criteria in rules adopted pursuant to chapter 91. "
