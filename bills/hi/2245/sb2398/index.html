A BILL FOR AN ACT 





RELATING TO RESIDENTIAL HOUSING UTILITIES . 





BE IT
ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII: 










SECTION 1. Chapter 54, Hawaii Revised Statutes, is
amended by adding a new section to part II to be appropriately designated and
to read as follows: 

" &sect;54- Service availability for residential dwellings. (a) The board of water supply shall publish on
its website a map of its service areas and provide a list of service-restricted
areas; provided that: 

(1) Parcel-by-parcel information shall
not be required; and 

(2) Utility assets need not be specified. 

(b) The map and list of service-restricted areas shall
be published on the website of the board of water supply no later than January
1, 2029, and shall be kept reasonably updated. 

(c) The board of water supply may include a
disclaimer stating that the map data is preliminary and does not constitute an
actual determination of water availability. 

(d) For water availability requests concerning
residentially zoned parcels of three or fewer units, the board shall respond in
writing to complete requests within forty-five days of receipt. Any determination under this subsection shall
be valid for ninety days from the date of issuance, unless otherwise specified
by the board of water supply. 

(e) The board of water supply may adopt rules
pursuant to chapter 91 necessary for the purposes of this section. "
