A BILL FOR AN ACT 





RELATING TO PROCUREMENT . 





BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII: 










SECTION 1. Chapter 103D, Hawaii Revised Statutes, is
amended by adding a new section to part I to be appropriately designated and to
read as follows: 

" &sect;103D- Project management services; authorization. (a) Notwithstanding any law to the contrary, governmental
bodies and public employees may procure project management services through
non-governmental third-party contracts; provided that: 

(1) All governmental agencies shall
retain ultimate and final authority and accountability for project management
as an inherently governmental responsibility; 

(2) Oversight of each project management
contract shall be performed by a designated public employee. Oversight shall include but not be limited to
monitoring performance, approving government documents, negotiating cost and
pricing, and awarding, modifying, and terminating contracts; and 

(3) Third-party project management
services may provide advisory, technical, or administrative support but shall
not exercise decision-making authority or supervise public employees. 

(b) Oversight of third-party
contracts shall remain an inherently governmental and nondelegable
responsibility of the state agency, which shall retain ultimate authority,
accountability, and decision-making over contract performance. " 

SECTION 2. 
Section 103D-104, Hawaii Revised Statutes, is amended by adding a new
definition to be appropriately inserted and to read as follows: 

" "Project
management" means a professional service that
manages a project's schedule, cost, quality, safety, scope, and function, and
ensures that the project is completed on time and within budget. "
