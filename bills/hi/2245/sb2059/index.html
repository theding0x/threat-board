BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII: 










SECTION 1. Chapter 139,
Hawaii Revised Statutes, is amended by adding a new section to be appropriately
designated and to read as follows: 

" &sect;139- Immigration
enforcement; delegation; prohibited. No
law enforcement agency shall enter into an agreement for the delegation of
immigration enforcement authority to any state or county agency pursuant to title
8 United States Code Section 1357. " 

SECTION 2. 
New statutory material is underscored. 

SECTION 3. 
This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
