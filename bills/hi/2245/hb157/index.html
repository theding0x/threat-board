HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES 


H.B. NO. 


157 




THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2025 


H.D. 1 




STATE OF HAWAII 


























A BILL FOR AN ACT 





RELATING TO TRANSPORTATION . 





BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII: 










PART I 

SECTION
1. The legislature finds that the roads
within the Villages of Kapolei, which were constructed by the Hawaii housing
finance and development corporation during the 1990s, were supposed to be
transferred to the city and county of Honolulu, but the transfer process has
stalled for decades due to the city and county of Honolulu establishing certain
requirements for accepting the dedication. 

The
purpose of this part is to require the Hawaii housing finance and development
corporation and the city and county of Honolulu to complete the transfer of the
Villages of Kapolei roads. 

SECTION
2. (a) 
The Hawaii housing finance and development corporation and the city and
county of Honolulu shall enter into an agreement for the transfer of the roads
within the Villages of Kapolei to the city and county of Honolulu. 

(b) The roads shall be transferred no later than
January 1, 2026. 

PART II 

SECTION
3. The purpose of this part is to
authorize county police officers to enforce the statewide traffic code on all
public streets, roadways, and highways within the State. 

SECTION
4. Chapter 291C, Hawaii Revised
Statutes, is amended by adding a new section to part I to be appropriately
designated and to read as follows: 

" &sect;291C- County police officer authority. Each county police officer may enforce this
chapter for any violation of this chapter that occurs on any federal, state,
and county street, roadway, or highway. " 

SECTION
5. New statutory material is
underscored. 

SECTION 6. This Act shall take effect on July 1, 3000. 














Report Title: 

Villages of Kapolei; Road Transfer; City and County of
Honolulu; HHFDC; Statewide Traffic Code 



Description: 

Requires
the Hawaii Housing Finance and Development Corporation to transfer the public
roads of the Villages of Kapolei to the City and County of Honolulu by 1/1/2026. Authorizes county police officers to enforce
the statewide traffic code on all public streets, roadways, and highways within
the State. Effective 7/1/3000. (HD1)
