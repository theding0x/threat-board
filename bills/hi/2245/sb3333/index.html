S.D. 1 




STATE OF HAWAII 


























A BILL FOR AN ACT 





RELATING TO TAXATION . 





BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII: 










SECTION 1. Chapter 46,
Hawaii Revised Statutes, is amended by adding a new section to be appropriately
designated and to read as follows: 

" &sect; 46- Affordable housing; real property tax
relief; date of transfer. Each
county shall apply any applicable real property tax exemptions, reduced
assessments, or tax classifications for any affordable housing subject to
income, resale, or occupancy restrictions as of the date a qualifying owner
takes title to the affordable housing for the upcoming tax period, consistent
with county procedures. " 

SECTION 2. 
New statutory material is underscored. 

SECTION 3. 
This Act shall take effect on April 19, 2042. 

















Report Title: 

Counties;
Real Property Tax Relief; Affordable Housing; Transfer Date 



Description: 

Requires each county to apply any applicable real property
tax exemptions, reduced assessments, or tax classifications for any affordable
housing subject to income, resale, or occupancy restrictions as of the date a
qualifying owner takes title to the affordable housing for the upcoming tax
period, consistent with county procedures. 
Effective 4/19/2042. (SD1)
