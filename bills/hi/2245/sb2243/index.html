A BILL FOR AN ACT 





relating
to cesspools . 





BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII: 










SECTION 1. The legislature finds that Act 125, Session
Laws of Hawaii 2017, requires the upgrade, conversion, or connection to a sewer
system of all cesspools statewide by 2050. 
Act 153, Session Laws of Hawaii 2022, subsequently recognized the classifications
of priority 1, priority 2, and priority 3 cesspools identified by the
university of Hawaii's 2021 Hawaii cesspool hazard assessment and prioritization
tool. 

The legislature further finds that
counties and homeowners face substantial financial, workforce, and
infrastructure limitations that make the current deadline unattainable. A revised, phased timeline will allow the
State and counties to address the highest-risk cesspools first while allowing
adequate time for planning and funding of long-term wastewater solutions. 

The purpose of this Act is to amend
the deadlines for the upgrade, conversion, or connection of cesspools by priority
classes, establishing December 31, 2050, as the date by which priority 1 cesspools
must be upgraded, converted, or connected, and phasing the deadlines for lower priority
cesspools in later years.
