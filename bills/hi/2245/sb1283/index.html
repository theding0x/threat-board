A BILL FOR AN ACT 





relating
to housing assistance . 





BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII: 










SECTION 1. The legislature finds that housing stability
is fundamental to the well-being of Hawaii's residents and overall health of
the State's economy. The State
recognizes the importance of timely and accessible financial assistance to
preserve homeownership, safeguard communities, and mitigate the broader
economic impact of foreclosure. The
homeowner assistance fund, established under the federal American Rescue Plan
Act of 2021, and other federal programs such as the emergency homeowners' loan
program and safeguarding tomorrow revolving loan fund program, provide valuable
frameworks for addressing the immediate needs of homeowners while promoting
long-term financial resilience. 

The legislature recognizes that there is a
pressing need for a state-managed revolving loan fund to provide emergency
mortgage and housing assistance that supports homeowners facing short-term
financial hardships. 

Accordingly, the purpose of this Act is to
establish the emergency home loan assistance revolving fund to provide Hawaii
residents loans, under certain conditions. 

SECTION
2. Chapter 201H, Hawaii Revised
Statutes, is amended by adding a new section to part III, subpart A to be
appropriately designated and to read as follows: 

" &sect;201H- Emergency home loan assistance revolving
fund. (a) 
There is established the emergency home loan assistance revolving fund
to be administered by the corporation. 

(b) The following may be deposited
into the fund: 

(1) Appropriations made by the
legislature; 

(2) Private contributions; 

(3) Proceeds from the repayment of loans
issued under this section; 

(4) Other returns; and 

(5) Moneys from other sources. 

(c) The fund shall be used to issue loans for
emergency home loan assistance payments to individuals who are in default or at
risk of being in default on their home loan; provided that a loan issued to an
individual shall not exceed an amount equal to three regular monthly payments
of the individual's home loan.
