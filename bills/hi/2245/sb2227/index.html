A BILL FOR AN ACT 





RELATING TO RENTAL ASSISTANCE . 





BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII: 










SECTION 1 . Chapter 356D, Hawaii Revised Statutes, is
amended by adding three new sections to part VIII to be appropriately
designated and to read as follows: 

" &sect;356D-A State rent
supplement program special fund. (a) 
There is established within the state treasury the state rent supplement
program special fund, to be administered by the authority and into which shall
be deposited: 

(1) The
transaction taxes collected pursuant to section 501- ; 

(2) The
transaction taxes collected pursuant to section 502- ; and 

(3) Legislative
appropriations. 

All interest earned and accrued on moneys, deposited
into the special fund shall become a part of the special fund. 

(b) Moneys from the state rent supplement program
special fund shall be used to: 

(1) Provide
rental assistance on behalf of qualified tenants under the state rent
supplement program established under part VIII of this chapter; and 

(2) Support
the functions of the state rent supplement program. 

&sect;356D-B State rent supplement program; memorandum
of agreement. The authority
may enter into memoranda of agreement with the counties or specialized
nonprofit organizations as necessary to implement this part. 

&sect;356D-C Rental supplement payments. (a) Subject to the availability of
funds, the authority shall award not less than six months and not more
than twelve months of supplemental rental payments on behalf of a qualified
tenant. Qualified tenants shall pay a
minimum of thirty per cent of their income towards rent. The authority shall subsidize the difference
between the established monthly rent for the qualified tenant's dwelling unit;
provided that the authority shall not pay more than $2,000 per month on behalf
of any qualified tenant. 

(b) 
Qualified tenants may be eligible for recertification under the program
pursuant to procedures adopted by the authority under section 356D-155(b). "
