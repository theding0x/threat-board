S.D. 1 




STATE OF HAWAII 

























SENATE RESOLUTION 





requesting the department of transportation, in
coordination with the hawaii housing finance and development corporation, to
convene a working group to evaluate the feasibility of establishing state-owned
or state-controlled warehouse and logistics facilities near ports, harbors, and
airports to reduce material costs associated with publicly financed housing
projects . 














WHEREAS, the
State continues to face a critical shortage of affordable housing, and the high
cost of construction materials significantly contributes to the overall cost of
publicly financed housing projects; and 



WHEREAS, Hawaii's
geographic isolation and reliance on imported building materials increase
transportation, storage, and handling costs; and 



WHEREAS, centralized
warehouse and logistics facilities located near ports, harbors, and airports
may allow the State to leverage bulk purchasing, improve supply chain
coordination, reduce delays, and stabilize material costs for publicly financed
housing developments; and 



WHEREAS, the
Department of Transportation oversees the State's airports, harbors, and
related transportation infrastructure and is well positioned to evaluate
potential sites and logistical considerations; and 



WHEREAS, the
Hawaii Housing Finance and Development Corporation administers programs that
finance and support the development of affordable housing statewide and can
provide expertise regarding housing development needs and cost drivers; and 



WHEREAS, collaboration
among relevant state agencies, housing developers, contractors, labor
representatives, and supply chain experts is necessary to assess the
feasibility, costs, benefits, governance structure, and potential funding
mechanisms for state-owned or state-controlled warehouse and logistics
facilities; now, therefore, 



BE IT
RESOLVED by the Senate of the Thirty-third Legislature of the State of Hawaii,
Regular Session of 2026, that the Department of Transportation, in coordination
with the Hawaii Housing Finance and Development Corporation, is requested to
convene a working group to evaluate the feasibility of establishing state-owned
or state-controlled warehouse and logistics facilities near ports, harbors, and
airports to reduce material costs associated with publicly financed housing
projects; and 



BE IT FURTHER
RESOLVED
