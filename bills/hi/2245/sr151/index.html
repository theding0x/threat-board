STATE OF HAWAII 

























SENATE RESOLUTION 





URGING THE HAWAII PUBLIC HOUSING AUTHORITY, DEPARTMENT OF
HUMAN SERVICES' HOMELESS PROGRAMS OFFICE, statewide OFFICE On HOMELESSNESS AND
HOUSING SOLUTIONS, AND HAWAII HOUSING FINANCE AND DEVELOPMENT CORPORATION TO
CREATE A HOUSING LADDER program TO help INDIVIDUALS AND FAMILIES GRADUATE INTO
UNSUBSIDIZED HOUSING . 












WHEREAS, the
State of Hawaii continues to face a significant shortage of affordable housing,
with demand for subsidized housing programs exceeding available units and
resources; and 



WHEREAS, thousands
of individuals and families remain on waiting lists for public housing, housing
vouchers, and other housing assistance programs; and 



WHEREAS, many
public housing and housing assistance programs are designed to provide
stability and a pathway toward greater self-sufficiency for program participants;
and 



WHEREAS, helping participants
who have achieved greater financial stability transition into market-rate or
non&#8209;subsidized housing can create opportunities for additional
individuals and families who are currently waiting for assistance; and 



WHEREAS, implementing
policies and programs that encourage and support upward mobility by means of a "housing
ladder" approach can help ensure that limited public resources are used
efficiently while maintaining stability for program participants; and 



WHEREAS, a
housing ladder and other "move-up" strategies may include financial
counseling, savings incentives, transitional rental assistance, tenant
education, workforce training, connection to critical behavioral and physical
health supportive services, and partnerships with private housing providers;
and 



WHEREAS, coordination
among the Department of Human Services' Homeless Programs Office, Statewide
Office on Homelessness and Housing Solutions, Hawaii Public Housing Authority,
and Hawaii Housing Finance and Development Corporation would help ensure a
comprehensive and coordinated strategy across the State's housing programs;
now, therefore, 



BE IT
RESOLVED by the Senate of the Thirty-third Legislature of the State of Hawaii,
Regular Session of 2026, that the Department of Human Services' Homeless
Programs Office, Statewide Office on Homelessness and Housing Solutions, Hawaii
