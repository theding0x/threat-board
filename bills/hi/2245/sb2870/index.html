THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2026 







STATE OF HAWAII 


























A BILL FOR AN ACT 





relating
to the Hawaii state lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, queer, plus commission . 





BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII: 










SECTION 1 . Section 369-3,
Hawaii Revised Statutes, is amended as follows: 

1. 
By amending subsection (b) to read: 

"(b) The commission shall consist of the
following: 

(1) Six ex officio,
nonvoting members or their designees, including the: 

(A) Superintendent of
education; 

(B) President of the
University of Hawaii system; 

(C) Director of labor
and industrial relations; 

(D) Director of human
resources development; 

(E) Director of human services;
and 

(F) Director of
health; and 

(2) [ Eight ] Nine 
voting members who shall be appointed by the governor in accordance with
section 26-34; provided that of these [ eight ] Nine members: 

(A) One member shall
be from the county of Hawaii; 

(B) One member shall
be from the county of Kauai; 

(C) One member shall
be from the county of Maui; 

(D) Four members shall
be from the city and county of Honolulu; [ and ] 

(E) One member shall
represent the Hawaii sexual and gender minority workgroup of the department of
health[ . ] ; and 

(F) One
member shall be under the age of twenty-nine at the time of their appointment;
and 

(3) Two voting
members, one of whom shall be appointed by the speaker of the house of
representatives and one of whom shall be appointed by the senate president. " 

2. 
By amending subsection (d) to read: 

" (d) A chairperson of the commission shall be
elected annually from the [ eight ] eleven appointed members of the
commission. A quorum to do business or
validate any act of the commission shall consist of a majority of the [ eight ]
eleven appointed members of the commission." 

SECTION 2. Statutory material to be repealed is
bracketed and stricken. New statutory
material is underscored. 

SECTION 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
