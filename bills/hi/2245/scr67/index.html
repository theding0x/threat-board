STATE OF HAWAII 

























SENATE CONCURRENT 

RESOLUTION 





requesting the department of transportation and
department of land and natural resources to coordinate the transfer of small
boat harbors back to the department of transportation and to establish a
management team to develop transition plans . 














WHEREAS, Act
272, Session Laws of Hawaii 1991 (Act 272), transferred the administration of
the State's small boat harbors from the Department of Transportation to the
Department of Land and Natural Resources; and 



WHEREAS, Act
272 included transition provisions requiring the establishment of a joint
management team to ensure continuity of operations, personnel alignment, and
administrative readiness during the transfer period; and 



WHEREAS, the Legislature
now seeks to return the small boat harbor program to the Department of
Transportation to better align harbor planning, capital programming,
operations, and maintenance with the State's integrated, multimodal
transportation system; and 



WHEREAS, the
transfer of authority, staff, operations, and assets requires a structured
transition process, sufficient lead time, and clear accountability to ensure
uninterrupted services and responsible stewardship of public resources; and 



WHEREAS, while
the transfer of full regulatory authority, responsibilities, and jurisdiction
over the State's small boat harbors would require statutory amendments through
the enactment of laws, the Legislature finds value in initiating coordinated
planning to ensure that any prospective transition proceeds in an orderly and
informed manner; now, therefore, 



BE IT
RESOLVED by the Senate of the Thirty-third Legislature of the State of Hawaii,
Regular Session of 2026, the House of Representatives concurring, that the
Department of Transportation and Department of Land and Natural Resources are
requested to coordinate the transfer of all authority, responsibilities,
positions, staff, operations, programs, assets, funds, and accounts associated
with the State's small boat harbors back to the Department of Transportation;
and 



BE IT FURTHER
RESOLVED that the Director of Transportation and Chairperson of the Board of
Land and Natural Resources are requested to establish a management team to
coordinate the transfer; and
