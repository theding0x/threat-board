A BILL FOR AN ACT 





RELATING TO THE DEPARTMENT OF BUSINESS, ECONOMIC
DEVELOPMENT, AND TOURISM . 





BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII: 










SECTION 1. The purpose of this Act is to rename the
department of business, economic development, and tourism as the department of
housing, economic development, and tourism to clarify that the department's
duties include the development of 
housing in the State and to emphasize that the department shall place
greater weight on its housing policy.
