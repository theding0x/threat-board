Training shall cover special permits, subdivision control, variances, fair 10housing and any other laws that govern the role and responsibility of said board. To the extent 
2 of 211practicable, the education, self-evaluation, and training programs shall be offered online and in 12various locations throughout the commonwealth, at various times of the year.13Every member of local planning boards, special permit granting authorities, and zoning 14boards of appeals shall, within 60 days after becoming a board member, and every 2 years 15thereafter, complete the education, self-evaluation, and training program, either online or in-16person. Upon completion of the program, the member shall provide notice of such completion to 17the town or city clerk, such notice to be retained for 6 years.
