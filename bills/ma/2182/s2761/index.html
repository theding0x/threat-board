Center for Housing Cooperatives3There is hereby established a center for housing cooperatives, herein called the center, 4within the executive office of housing and livable communities to promote housing cooperatives 5for low to moderate income people in the commonwealth. The center shall provide education, 6conduct outreach and promote efforts to: (i) expand and enhance cooperative resident ownership; 7(ii) preserve the existing and increase the number of cooperatively owned properties; (iii) 8publicize and promote the benefits of resident cooperative housing to policy makers and the 9general public; (iv) encourage collaborative outreach efforts regarding involvement and 10cooperative housing ownership; (v) research and evaluate cooperative housing ownership in the 11commonwealth; (vi) showcase cooperative housing initiatives in the commonwealth; (vii) 12facilitate and coordinate the sharing of existing information and resources; and (viii) provide 13grants pursuant to this chapter.
