The executive office may designate additional 7municipalities as seasonal communities based on consideration of the following factors: (i) a 8high rate of short-term rentals in relation to the overall housing inventory; (ii) a significant 9population increase in seasonal visitors; (iii) an excessive disparity between the area median 10income and the income required to purchase the municipality's median home price; (iv) the 11percentage of housing stock that is used for seasonal, occasional or recreational use or is
