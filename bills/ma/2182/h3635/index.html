In order to qualify for the reduction, the driver must be age 65 and over, the 6driver must have a valid driver’s license recognized by the Registry of Motor Vehicles as a valid 7license to drive in Massachusetts, and the driver must own or rent the passenger vehicle.  The 8driver is only allowed to apply the reduction to one passenger vehicle, which the driver must 9own or rent.  There shall be no application fee for a driver to apply and be approved for said
