Transportation.The Commonwealth of Massachusetts_______________In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court(2025-2026)_______________An Act relative to 988 Samaritan suicide prevention signs on bridges of the commonwealth.Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority of the same, as follows:1SECTION 1          Notwithstanding any general or special law, rule or regulation to the 2contrary, the department of highways shall install signage on all bridges of the commonwealth 3under its jurisdiction. There shall be two signs on each bridge at either end of said bridges. The 4signs shall contain "988 Suicide Prevention" and any other information necessary to convey to 5individuals that an alternative to suicide exists and help is available by calling said number.  6Such signs shall be of a reasonable size that is easily readable by either pedestrians or drivers 7using the bridge as determined by the department of highways. 8SECTION 2          Signage on all commonwealth bridges shall be installed by the 9department of highways within one year of the passage of this act.
