No. 5149The Commonwealth of Massachusetts______________________________________                            HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES, December 23, 2024.                          The committee on Ways and Means, to whom was referred the Bill relative to the creation of a branch of the Boston Public Library within an affordable housing development in the Dorchester section of the City of Boston (House, No. 4432), reports recommending that the same ought to pass with an amendment substituting therefor the accompanying bill (House, No. 5149).For the committee,AARON MICHLEWITZ.

No. 5149The Commonwealth of Massachusetts_______________In the One Hundred and Ninety-Third General Court(2023-2024)_______________An Act relative to the creation of a branch of the Boston public library within an affordable housing development in the Dorchester section of the city of Boston.Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority of the same, as follows:1SECTION 1. Construction and development activity related to redevelopment by the 2developer selected by the Boston Redevelopment Authority of subsidized affordable housing, 3including space for a branch library to be procured by the Boston public library, to be built on 4Boston Redevelopment Authority-owned land under a ground lease, or any part thereof, shall not 5be subject to any general or special law related to the procurement and award of contracts for the 6planning, design, construction management, construction, reconstruction, installation, 7demolition, maintenance or repair of buildings by a public agency, but shall be subject to 8sections 26 to 27H, inclusive, of chapter 149 of the Generals Laws. Contracts for the 9construction, reconstruction, alteration, remodeling or repair of any publicly owned public works 10that service this project and would otherwise be subject to section 39M of chapter 30 of the 11General Laws shall be subject to said section 39M if the redevelopment of the project is funded 12in part by state or federal government low-income housing tax credit, grant or loan, or pursuant 13to the issuance of tax-exempt bonds authorized by general law. A conveyance of the project, 
3 of 314whether by leasehold or fee estate, to an urban redevelopment corporation organized under 15chapter 121A of the General Laws or to a nonprofit state and federally tax-exempt corporation 16organized for the purpose of revitalizing the project, shall be subject to chapter 30B of the 17General Laws to the extent that the project is conveyed to an entity that is not owned, controlled 18or managed by the Boston public library on the date of the conveyance.19SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon its passage.
