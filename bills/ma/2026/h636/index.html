For the purposes of administering the federal Older Americans Act of 1965 3and subsequent amendments thereto, the term "greatest social need", as defined in 42 U.S.C. 43002(24), shall mean the need caused by non-economic factors that restrict an individual's ability 5to perform normal daily tasks or that threaten the individual’s capacity to live independently. 6These factors shall include, but are not limited to, physical or mental disability, language barriers 7and cultural or social isolation caused by, among other things, racial and ethnic status, sexual 8orientation, gender identity or expression or HIV status.
