Housing.The Commonwealth of Massachusetts_______________In the One Hundred and Ninety-Third General Court(2023-2024)_______________An Act relative to year-round housing.Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority of the same, as follows:1Any property that meets the minimum square footage for habitability under the state 2sanitary code shall be permitted to be used as year-round housing. This section 7shall preempt any local bylaw that prohibits the year-round habitability of property as set forth 8herein.
