Housing.The Commonwealth of Massachusetts_______________In the One Hundred and Ninety-Third General Court(2023-2024)_______________An Act relative to emergency backup power in newly-constructed senior housing facilities.Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority of the same, as follows:1SECTION 1. Notwithstanding any general or special law to the contrary, every senior 2housing facility newly-constructed after the effective date of this act shall be required to be 3equipped with emergency backup electric generators prior to the issuance of any occupancy 4permit by a city or town. For the purposes of this act, the term "senior housing facility" shall be 5defined as any residential property with four or more dwelling units that is specifically intended 6to serve as the primary residence for persons aged 55 years and older, and shall include but not 7be limited to supportive housing, shared living in congregate settings, and continuing care 8communities.
