(a) There shall be established and set up on the books of the 3commonwealth a grant program known as the Mobile Shower Program that shall be administered 4by the Department of Housing and Community Development. The purpose of this program shall 5be to assist municipalities and nonprofits to build and maintain mobile showers.  Mobile showers 6are to be defined as public showers that are transportable, either by towing or by loading onto a 7truck/vehicle, with the primary purpose of assisting homeless populations with sanitation 8services. They are to be used primarily for the purpose of assisting homeless populations, 9including youth. The placement of mobile showers will be prioritized to serve underserved 10communities across the commonwealth.  11(b) There shall be credited to the fund all amounts that are transferred, or authorized to be 12transferred thereto, or directed to be deposited therein, and all amounts received as gifts, grants,
