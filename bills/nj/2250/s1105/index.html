STATEMENT 



This bill provides a
supplemental appropriation of $600,000 for Joseph's House of Camden. The
purpose of the supplemental appropriation is to more adequately support
Joseph's House of Camden in its endeavor to provide shelter, and programs and
services, for people experiencing homelessness.
