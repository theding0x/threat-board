An Act authorizing fair share obligation bonus credit
for affordable housing reserved for teachers and supplementing P.L.1985, c.222
(C.52:27D-301 et al.). 



Be It
Enacted by the Senate and General Assembly of
the State of New Jersey: 



1. a. For the purposes of
obtaining credit towards a municipality's fair share obligation, a municipality
shall obtain one credit and one-half bonus credit for each unit of low- or
moderate-income housing occupied or reserved for occupancy by a low- or
moderate-income household with at least one member of the household who is
employed as a teacher at the time of initial occupancy of the unit. 

b. As used in this section,
"teacher" means a person employed in a public or non-public school to teach in
preschool through grade 12. 



2. This act shall take effect
immediately. 





STATEMENT 



This bill authorizes
municipalities to obtain fair share affordable housing bonus credit for certain
housing reserved for occupancy by low- and moderate-income households with at
least one member of the household who is employed as a teacher. The bill would
specifically permit a municipality to obtain one credit and one-half bonus
credit for each unit of affordable housing reserved for occupancy by a low- or
moderate-income teacher. The bill defines a teacher as a person employed in a
public or non-public school to teach in preschool through grade 12. The bill
would take effect immediately.
