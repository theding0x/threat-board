An Act concerning Medicaid beneficiaries. 



Be It
Enacted by the Senate and General Assembly of
the State of New Jersey: 



1. a. Within 180 days of the
effective date of this act, Commissioner of Human Services shall issue guidance
to the Division of Medical Assistance and Health Services in the Department of
Human Services, public housing agencies, Continuums of Care, and housing
finance agencies to connect individuals who are receiving medical assistance
under the Medicaid program established pursuant to P.L.1968, c.413 (C.30:4D-1
et seq.) to Medicaid housing-related services and supports and other housing
resources in an integrated manner. At a minimum, such guidance shall include
information that: 

(1) establishes, or
describes, the State's framework for coordinated interagency housing-services assistance
under the Medicaid program; 

(2) simplifies health care
services for housing and homelessness service providers and simplifying housing
support services for health care service providers to understand the respective
sectors; 

(3) provides guidance with
respect to tracking common outcome measures, including housing stability rates
and health outcomes disaggregated by race; and 

(4) describes existing
authorities under which the Medicaid program may make funds available to
providers of housing-related services and supports to build information
technology and financial systems and establish adequate infrastructure and
staffing levels to seek Medicaid reimbursement. 

b. Within 12 months, the
division shall develop a memorandum of understanding between the division,
public housing agencies, Continuums of Care, and housing finance agencies that: 

(1) coordinates
housing-related services and supports provided under the Medicaid program with
other housing resources by aligning eligibility criteria, eligibility
determination processes, and outcome measures, including housing stability and
health outcomes disaggregated by race; and 

(2) encourages providers of
housing-related services and supports to seek reimbursement for such services
and supports through the Medicaid program, including by: 

(a) explaining how such
providers may receive such reimbursement; 

(b) helping such providers
understand the eligibility determination process for housing-related services
and supports offered under the Medicaid program.
