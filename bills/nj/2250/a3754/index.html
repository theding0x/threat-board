SYNOPSIS 

Clarifies and expands landlord registration
procedures; creates certain rights for tenants; makes certain changes
concerning tenant notifications, inspections, and maintenance in multiple
dwellings; permits percentage of affordable housing obligation to be satisfied
by certain rehabilitation projects.
