STATEMENT 



This bill would appropriate up
to $10,000,000 to the Department of Transportation for the purpose of
installing highway guide rail along the entirety of Route 208 through Franklin
Lakes, Wyckoff, Hawthorne, Glen Rock, Fair Lawn, and Oakland in sections that
do not already have highway guard rail installed, where necessary to ensure the
health and safety of motorists and residents whose properties abut Route 208.
