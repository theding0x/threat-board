An Act relating to federal housing choice voucher program
administration and supplementing chapter 27D of Title 52 of the Revised
Statutes. 



Be It
Enacted by the Senate and General Assembly of
the State of New Jersey: 



1. The Department of
Community Affairs shall establish a standard initial period of at least 180
calendar days for a household in this State to locate new housing under the
federal Housing Choice Voucher Program, established pursuant to 42 U.S.C.
1437f, if the household is required to vacate their rental unit as a result of
the sale or transfer of the property by the landlord. The standard initial
term shall be stated on the voucher. The standard initial period established
by the department pursuant to this section shall apply to each qualified Public
Housing Agency within the meaning of 42 U.S.C. 1437f and 24 C.F.R. s.5.100 in
the State, including a housing authority established pursuant to section 17 of
P.L.1992, c.79 (C.40A:12A-17). The department, in its role as a qualified
Public Housing Agency within the meaning of 42 U.S.C. 1437f and 24 C.F.R.
s.5.100, may grant a household one or more extensions of the initial period. 



2. The Commissioner of
Community Affairs shall promulgate rules and regulations, in accordance with
the "Administrative Procedure Act," P.L.1968, c.410 (C.52:14B-1, et
seq.), that are necessary to effectuate the provisions of P.L. , c. 
(C. ) (pending before the Legislature as this bill), which shall include
changes to the department's administrative plan pursuant to 24 C.F.R. s.982.54
that are consistent with P.L. , c. (C. ) (pending before the
Legislature as this bill). 



3. This act shall take effect
on the first day of the seventh month next following enactment, except that the
Commissioner of Community Affairs may take such anticipatory action as may be
necessary to implement the provisions of P.L. , c. (C. ) (pending
before the Legislature as this bill).
