STATEMENT 



This bill supplements the
Fiscal Year 2026 appropriations act by providing an additional $4 million in
funding for Hinchliffe Stadium Partners to support the development and
maintenance of sports, entertainment, and tourism infrastructure in the
Paterson Great Falls National Park. This funding would supplement a $900,000
appropriation made for Hinchliffe Stadium Partners in Fiscal Year 2026.
