1. R.S.32:16-1 is amended
to read as follows: 

32:16-1. a. In
addition to the powers conferred by this subtitle, but except as otherwise
provided in this section, the Palisades Interstate Park Commission is
authorized to construct, maintain and operate a parkway on top of the
Palisades, extending from the southerly line of the park already established to
the New York State line and within one thousand feet westerly from the top or
steep edge of the Palisades or the crest of the slope in places where the steep
Palisades rocks are absent, and, for such purpose and the acquisition of the
necessary lands therefor, may apply for and accept grants or other aid from the
Federal emergency administration of public works, or other boards, officers or
departments of the United States. 

b. For the purpose
of laying out and establishing the parkway mentioned in this section, the
commission may acquire by gift, purchase or condemnation any lands or rights in
lands necessary for such objects and lying within the area described in this
section; but, after a copy of the map required to be made by [ section
32:16-2 of this Title ]

county pursuant to [ section
32:16-3 of this Title ] 
R.S.32:16-3 , the right to condemn lands shall not extend west of the
westerly boundary line of the parkway as shown on the map.

Department of Transportation shall assume responsibility for the operations and
maintenance of the Palisades Interstate Parkway and shall assume responsibility

responsibilities shall be limited to those portions of the parkway that are
located within this State.
