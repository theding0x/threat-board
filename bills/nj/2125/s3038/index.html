An Act concerning New Jersey Transit Corporation fares and
supplementing P.L.1979, c.150 (C.27:25-1 et al.). 



Be It
Enacted by the Senate and General Assembly of
the State of New Jersey: 



1. a. As used in P.L. ,
c. (C. ) (pending before the Legislature as this bill): 

"New Jersey Transit
Corporation service" or "corporation service" means motorbus regular route
service and rail passenger service, including light rail service, operated by
or under contract to the New Jersey Transit Corporation. 

"Work First New Jersey"
means the Work First New Jersey temporary public assistance program for
dependent children and their parents established pursuant to P.L.1997, c.38
(C.44:10-55 et seq.). 

"Workforce training program"
means an unpaid apprenticeship, internship, or other learning program that
provides training and real workplace experience in a particular field. 

b. Notwithstanding any law,
rule, or regulation to the contrary, the New Jersey Transit Corporation shall
offer a discount rate when purchasing a ticket or pass for New Jersey Transit
Corporation service, to: 

(1) elementary and secondary
school students who use corporation service to commute to and from school; 

(2) full-time college and
university students who use corporation service to commute to and from school; 

(3) full-time workforce
training program students who use corporation service to commute to and from a
workforce training program; 

(4) individuals currently receiving
benefits under Work First New Jersey; and 

(5) a newly employed
individual who meets the following criteria: 

(a) commutes to and from the
individual's place of employment using corporation service; 

(b) has a starting salary
equivalent to the State minimum wage; 

(c) is at least 18 years of
age or older and not a full-time student; 

(d) works a minimum of 20
hours per week; 

(e) has been unemployed at
least 30 days before taking the new position; 

(f) is not currently receiving
public assistance from a Work First New Jersey; and 

(g) is a resident of New
Jersey. 



2. This act shall take
effect immediately.
