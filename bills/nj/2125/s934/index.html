An Act 
concerning the display of the American flag on bridges or overpasses that
travel over property of the New Jersey Turnpike Authority and South Jersey
Transportation Authority and supplementing Title 27 of the Revised Statutes. 



Be It
Enacted by the Senate and General Assembly of
the State of New Jersey: 



1. Notwithstanding the
provisions of any other law, rule, or regulation to the contrary, a person may
display an American flag on a bridge or overpass that travels over property of
the New Jersey Turnpike Authority, provided that the person receives permission
from the authority through a permit process established by the authority and
the flag is properly maintained by the person and displayed in a manner
consistent with the rules and customs deemed the proper manner to display the
flag, such as the federal flag code, 4 U.S.C. s.1 et seq., or any other
applicable law or guideline. Notwithstanding the provisions of any other law
to the contrary, the New Jersey Turnpike Authority shall not remove any
American flag that is displayed in accordance with this section. 



2. Notwithstanding the
provisions of any other law, rule, or regulation to the contrary, a person may
display an American flag on a bridge or overpass that travels over property of
the South Jersey Transportation Authority, provided that the person receives
permission from the authority through a permit process established by the
authority and the flag is properly maintained by the person and displayed in a
manner consistent with the rules and customs deemed the proper manner to
display the flag, such as the federal flag code, 4 U.S.C. s.1 et seq., or any
other applicable law or guideline. Notwithstanding the provisions of any other
law to the contrary, the South Jersey Transportation Authority shall not remove
any American flag that is displayed in accordance with this section. 



3. This act shall take
effect immediately.
