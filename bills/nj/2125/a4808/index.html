NJ A4808 — Revises registered apprenticeship requisites of public works contractors and creates opt-out from participation in registered apprenticeship program for public works projects.

INTROCMTEFLOORPASSEDLAW
WHY IT WAS FLAGGED

Bill regulates apprenticeship requirements and creates an opt-out fee mechanism for public works contractors, but does not directly increase or decrease public works funding; it redistributes apprenticeship-related fees to labor development and small business grants rather than funding infrastructure projects themselves.
