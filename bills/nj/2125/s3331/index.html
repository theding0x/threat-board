STATEMENT 



This bill would appropriate
$12 million from the General Fund to the Village of Ridgewood in Bergen County
to offset the costs of infrastructure improvements that are needed to mitigate
per- and poly-fluoroalkyl substance (PFAS) pollution in the drinking water
system. 

The Ridgewood Water
Department, also known as Ridgewood Water, is a public community water system
that serves over 60,000 residents in Ridgewood, Midland Park, Glen Rock, and
Wyckoff. The sponsor's intent in introducing the legislation is to avoid the
significant rate increases that Ridgewood Water would otherwise need to impose
on its ratepayers in order to fund the PFAS-related infrastructure
improvements.
