NJ A4981 — Permits court to take additional time to consider pretrial release or pretrial detention when firearm offense is involved.

WHY IT WAS FLAGGED

The bill tightens firearm-related restrictions by allowing courts additional time (up to 7 days) to conduct ballistic analysis for defendants charged with firearm offenses, and explicitly permits non-monetary conditions including prohibition on possessing firearms, thereby adding gun control measures in the pretrial detention context.
