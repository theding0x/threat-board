SB3187 Introduced 2/6/2024, by Sen. Neil Anderson SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED:
430 ILCS 65/5 from Ch. 38, par. 83-5 430 ILCS 66/50 Amends the Firearm Owners Identification Card Act. Provides that a renewed Firearm Owner's Identification Card issued under the Act shall be valid for the person to whom it is issued for a period of 10 years from the date of expiration on the previously issued card. Amends the Firearm Concealed Carry Act. Provides that a renewed license under the Act shall be valid for the person to whom it is issued for a period of 5 years from the date of expiration of the previously issued license. Effective immediately.
