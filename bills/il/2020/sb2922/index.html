SB2922 Introduced 1/26/2024, by Sen. Michael W. Halpin SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED:
35 ILCS 5/241 new Amends the Illinois Income Tax Act. Provides a tax credit for certain developers of single-family residences that incur development costs and that sell or rent qualified residences to individuals who meet certain income thresholds. Sets forth the amount of the credit. Effective immediately.
