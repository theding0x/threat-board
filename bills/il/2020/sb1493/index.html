Amends the Project Labor Agreements Act. Provides for project labor agreements on public works projects totaling $15,000,000 or more in costs. Effective immediately.

7 Sec. 10. Public works projects. On projects totaling 8 $15,000,000 or more in costs a project-by-project basis , a 9 State department, agency, authority, board, or instrumentality 10 that is under the control of the Governor shall , on a 11 project-by-project basis, include a project labor agreement on 12 a public works project when that department, agency, 13 authority, board, or instrumentality has determined that the 14 agreement advances the State's interests of cost, efficiency, 15 quality, safety, timeliness, skilled labor force, labor 16 stability, or the State's policy to advance minority-owned and 17 women-owned businesses and minority and female employment. For 18 purposes of this Act, any corrective action performed pursuant 19 to Title XVI of the Environmental Protection Act for which 20 payment from the Underground Storage Tank Fund is requested 21 shall be considered a public works project.
