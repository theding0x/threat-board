SB3569 Introduced 2/9/2024, by Sen. Erica Harriss SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED:
5 ILCS 420/3A-55 new 5 ILCS 430/99-15 new 20 ILCS 3805/36 new Amends the Illinois Housing Development Act. Creates the Fairness in Property Tax Foreclosure Task Force. Sets forth the membership of the Task Force. Provides that the Task Force shall study and make recommendations concerning issues associated with property foreclosure for failure to pay property taxes. Amends the Illinois Governmental Ethics Act and the State Officials and Employees Ethics Act to make conforming changes. Effective immediately.
