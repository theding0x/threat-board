HB5626 Introduced 2/9/2024, by Rep. Rita Mayfield SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED:
605 ILCS 140/5 Amends the Expressway Camera Act. Provides that funds needed to conduct the program for use on expressways under the jurisdiction of the Illinois State Toll Highway Authority may be used for equipment, installation, service, and maintenance of the camera systems, telecommunication costs, and for camera warranties.
