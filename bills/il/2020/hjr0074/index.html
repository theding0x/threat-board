2 WHEREAS, It is highly fitting that the Illinois General 3 Assembly pays honor and respect to veterans who have served 4 their country and the State of Illinois; and
5 WHEREAS, Staff Sergeant Wilbur E. Troehler was born in the 6 small farming town of Fairbury to parents Grover C. and Aline 7 Troehler on March 3, 1916; he married Ruth M. Urban on February 8 21, 1941, and they settled in Chicago, where they had three 9 sons, Eugene, William, and Robert; and
10 WHEREAS, SSG Troehler was inducted into the United States 11 Army at Gary, Indiana on March 23, 1943; he received his basic 12 training at Fort Leonard Wood, Missouri and advanced 13 individual training at Camp Breckenridge, Kentucky; upon 14 completion of training, he was assigned to the 75th Infantry 15 Division and further assigned to Company D, 289th Infantry 16 Regiment; the 75th Infantry Division was activated April 15, 17 1943 and deployed overseas on November 14, 1944; and
18 WHEREAS, SSG Troehler entered combat as a Heavy Weapons 19 NCO and Section Leader under the 75th Infantry Division on 20 Christmas Day 1944 in the Ardennes Counter Offensive, later 21 known as the Battle of the Bulge; and
