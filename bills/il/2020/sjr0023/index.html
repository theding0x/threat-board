1 SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION
2 WHEREAS, It is highly fitting that the Illinois General 3 Assembly pays honor and respect to the truly great individuals 4 who have served our country and State and, in doing so, have 5 made the ultimate sacrifice; and
6 WHEREAS, Corporal Donald Claude Taylor was born on August 7 30, 1949; and
8 WHEREAS, Cpl. Taylor served with the U.S. Army, 11th Light 9 Infantry Brigade, 4th Battalion, 3rd Infantry, C Company; and
10 WHEREAS, Cpl. Taylor began his tour of duty in Vietnam on 11 December 11, 1969; and
12 WHEREAS, Cpl. Taylor gave his life in service to our 13 country on March 5, 1970; and
14 WHEREAS, Cpl. Taylor was awarded the Purple Heart, the 15 Combat Infantryman Badge, the Marksmanship Badge, the National 16 Defense Service Medal, the Vietnam Campaign Medal, the Vietnam 17 Service Medal, the Army Presidential Unit Citation, the 18 Vietnam Gallantry Cross, and the Army Good Conduct Medal; and
19 WHEREAS, Cpl. Taylor's sacrifice is honored on the Vietnam SJ0023 - 2 - LRB103 28883 MST 55269 r
1 Veteran's Memorial in Washington, DC; his name can be found 2 inscribed at VVM Wall, Panel 13w, Line 86; therefore, be it
3 RESOLVED, BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED THIRD GENERAL 4 ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ILLINOIS, THE HOUSE OF 5 REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING HEREIN, that we designate 6 Interstate 55 as it travels around the city of Chenoa as the 7 "Cpl. Donald C. Taylor Memorial Highway"; and be it further
8 RESOLVED, That the Illinois Department of Transportation 9 is requested to erect at suitable locations, consistent with 10 State and federal regulations, appropriate plaques or signs 11 giving notice of the name "Cpl. Donald C. Taylor Memorial 12 Highway"; and be it further
13 RESOLVED, That suitable copies of this resolution be 14 presented to the family of Cpl. Taylor and the Secretary of the 15 Illinois Department of Transportation.
