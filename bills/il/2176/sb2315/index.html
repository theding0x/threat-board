SB2315 Introduced 2/7/2025, by Sen. Laura Ellman SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED:
30 ILCS 425/4 from Ch. 127, par. 2804 Amends the Build Illinois Bond Act. Authorizes bonds to be issued under the Act for the purpose of making of grants to units of local government, including counties and park districts, through the Illinois Green Infrastructure Grant Program to restore and protect shorelines in the State.
