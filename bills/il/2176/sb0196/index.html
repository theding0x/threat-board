SB0196 Introduced 1/22/2025, by Sen. Chapin Rose SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED:
765 ILCS 745/12 from Ch. 80, par. 212 765 ILCS 745/14.4 new Amends the Mobile Home Landlord and Tenant Rights Act. Prohibits a landlord from imposing a rule, regulation, or lease provision, in any manner, that impairs any rights guaranteed to the tenant by the First Amendment of the Constitution of the United States or Section 4 of Article I of the Illinois Constitution, including the display of advertising pertaining to any political candidate or proposition.
