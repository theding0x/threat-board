HB3414 Introduced , by Rep. Amy Elik SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED:
210 ILCS 9/92 new Amends the Assisted Living and Shared Housing Establishments Act. Provides that an establishment must report to the Department of Public Health any incident or accident that results in significant physical harm or injury to a resident or any situation where a resident requires immediate medical attention, including admission to the hospital, as a direct result of an incident or accident. Provides that a change in a resident's condition that is due to health or medical decline is not a reportable incident or accident.
