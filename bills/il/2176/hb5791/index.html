2025 and 2026 HB5791 Introduced , by Rep. Jed Davis SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: New Act Creates the Family First Housing Act. Provides that an owner-occupied residential property may contain one accessory dwelling unit for occupancy by a parent of the property owner or the property owner's spouse. Sets forth parameters of accessory dwelling units for family care. Defines terms. Effective January 1, 2027.
