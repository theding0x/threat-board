HB1610 Introduced , by Rep. Maura Hirschauer SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED:
310 ILCS 10/35 new Amends the Housing Authorities Act. Requires every housing authority to post on its official website in a downloadable, printable format sample rental or lease agreements that are written in the 3 most common non-English languages spoken in the city, village, incorporated town, or county for which the housing authority was created.
