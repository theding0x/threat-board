HB1247 Introduced , by Rep. Dave Vella SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED:
820 ILCS 130/2 Amends the Prevailing Wage Act. Provides that the definition of "public works" includes sewer inspection projects that utilize a closed-circuit television to identify issues in a sewer system, such as cracks in pipes, root intrusion, blockages, or other structural damage.
