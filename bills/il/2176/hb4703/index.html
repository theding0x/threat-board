2025 and 2026 HB4703 Introduced , by Rep. Dave Vella SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: New Act 35 ILCS 5/246 new Creates the Short Line Railroad Modernization Act. Creates an income tax credit for taxpayers that incur qualified railroad expenditures or qualified new rail infrastructure expenditures. Sets forth the amount of the credit and limitations on the amount of the credit that may be awarded. Amends the Illinois Income Tax Act to make conforming changes. Effective immediately.
