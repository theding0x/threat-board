2025 and 2026 SB3612 Introduced 2/5/2026, by Sen. Rachel Ventura SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 220 ILCS 5/16-108.35 new Amends the Public Utilities Act. Provides that, beginning January 1, 2027, broadband providers shall offer affordable rates to serve the needs of low-income broadband customers. Provides that the Illinois Commerce Commission shall establish and implement appropriate rules to ensure that low-income broadband rates are developed by broadband providers, including, but not limited to, rules establishing minimum Internet service speeds at affordable rates and setting a maximum low-income broadband rate for broadband service. Effective immediately.
