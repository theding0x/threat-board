1 AN ACT concerning regulation.
2 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Illinois, 3 represented in the General Assembly:
4 Section 5. The Assisted Living and Shared Housing Act is 5 amended by adding Section 111 as follows:
6 (210 ILCS 9/111 new) 7 Sec. 111. Joint provider, surveyor, and association 8 trainings. The Department shall semiannually provide joint 9 training sessions for surveyors and assisted living 10 establishment providers. The Department shall include the 11 State long-term care ombudsman, or the State long-term care 12 ombudsman's designee, and representatives of each assisted 13 living provider association in the State in the planning 14 process to create the topics and content of the joint training 15 sessions as well as the coordination and presentations for the 16 joint training sessions. At least annually, a joint training 17 session shall include, but not be limited to, regional 18 citation patterns relating to complaints, standards, and 19 outcomes in the assisted living survey process. The Department 20 shall also develop standardized training for establishments to 21 prevent common citations in the assisted living survey 22 process.
