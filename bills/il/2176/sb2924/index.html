2025 and 2026 SB2924 Introduced 1/27/2026, by Sen. Neil Anderson SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED: 520 ILCS 5/3.1-6 Amends the Wildlife Code. In provisions regarding special deer, turkey, and combination hunting licenses, provides that the deer, turkey, or combination hunting permit issued without fee shall be valid on all farm lands year-round which the person to whom it is issued owns, leases, or rents, with other requirements. Provides that resident landowners shall be entitled to up to 5 resident deer archery either-sex permits and up to 5 resident deer either-sex firearm permits without charge.
