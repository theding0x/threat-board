HB2616 Introduced , by Rep. Adam M. Niemerg SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED:
55 ILCS 5/3-3013 from Ch. 34, par. 3-3013 Amends the Counties Code. Provides that, when a corner knows or is informed that a death is suspected to be a maternal or fetal death due to an abortion, the coroner shall go to the place where the dead body is located, take charge of the body, and make a preliminary investigation into the circumstances of the death. Effective immediately.
