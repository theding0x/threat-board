SB0088 Introduced 1/17/2025, by Sen. Mike Simmons SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED:
310 ILCS 65/3 from Ch. 67 1/2, par. 1253 310 ILCS 65/8 from Ch. 67 1/2, par. 1258 Amends the Illinois Affordable Housing Act. Provides that funds in the Illinois Affordable Housing Trust Fund may be used for housing counseling. Defines "housing counseling" to mean services provided by an organization approved by the United States Department of Housing and Urban Development to be a HUD-Approved Housing Counseling Agency.
