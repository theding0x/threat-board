430 ILCS 65/5 from Ch. 38, par. 83-5 430 ILCS 65/7 from Ch. 38, par. 83-7 430 ILCS 65/13.2 from Ch. 38, par. 83-13.2 430 ILCS 65/14 from Ch. 38, par. 83-14 430 ILCS 66/10 430 ILCS 66/45 430 ILCS 66/50 430 ILCS 66/60 430 ILCS 66/70 430 ILCS 66/75 Amends the Firearm Owners Identification Card Act. Provides that the duration of a Firearm Owner's Identification Card shall be the lifetime of the holder of the Card (rather than 10 years). Amends the Firearm Concealed Carry Act. Provides that the duration of a concealed carry license is the lifetime of the licensee (rather than 5 years). Provides that a Firearm Owner's Identification Card or concealed carry license issued before the effective date of the amendatory Act shall be valid during the Card holder's or licensee's lifetime regardless of the expiration date on the Card or license.
