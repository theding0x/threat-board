SB1951 Introduced 2/6/2025, by Sen. Linda Holmes SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED:
5 ILCS 100/5-45.65 new 520 ILCS 5/1.4 from Ch. 61, par. 1.4 520 ILCS 5/2.30 from Ch. 61, par. 2.30 Amends the Wildlife Code. Provides that it shall be unlawful for any person to trap or to hunt gray fox except during an open season, which may (rather than will) be set annually by the Director of Natural Resources between 12:01 a.m., November 1 to 12:00 midnight, February 15, both inclusive. Allows the Director, by administrative rule, to set the dates of, lengthen, shorten, or close the season during which gray fox may be taken. Allows the Director to also set, lessen, or increase the daily bag limit or possession limit of gray fox by administrative rule. Amends the Illinois Administrative Procedure Act. Grants the Department of Natural Resources emergency rulemaking powers. Effective immediately.
