HB1147 Introduced , by Rep. Dagmara Avelar SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED:
New Act 35 ILCS 5/246 new 215 ILCS 5/409 from Ch. 73, par. 1021 215 ILCS 5/444 from Ch. 73, par. 1056 Creates the Build Illinois Homes Tax Credit Act. Provides that owners of qualified low-income housing developments are eligible for credits against the taxes imposed by the Illinois Income Tax Act or taxes, penalties, fees, charges, and payments imposed by the Illinois Insurance Code. Amends the Illinois Income Tax Act and the Illinois Insurance Code to make conforming changes. Effective immediately.
