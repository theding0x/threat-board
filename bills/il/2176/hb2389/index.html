HB2389 Introduced , by Rep. Terra Costa Howard SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED:
430 ILCS 65/7 from Ch. 38, par. 83-7 Amends the Firearm Owners Identification Card Act. Provides that if a Firearm Owner's Identification Card is issued to a holder under 21 years of age, then that Firearm Owner's Identification Card shall expire on the holder's 21st birthday or 10 years from the date of issuance, whichever is earlier. Provides that after the holder's 21st birthday, the validity period of the holder's Firearm Owner's Identification Card shall be 10 years.
