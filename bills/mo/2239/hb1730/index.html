WHY IT WAS FLAGGED

Bill decriminalizes firearm suppressors in Missouri (removes them from the prohibited weapons list as of Aug 28, 2026) and prevents enforcement of federal suppressor regulations on in-state manufactured suppressors, directly loosening firearm restrictions and expanding gun rights.
