MO HB2064 — Repeals provisions that prohibit political subdivisions from adopting orders, ordinances, or regulations relating to firearms

PRO SPONSORS: 1 D
INTROCMTEFLOORPASSEDLAW
WHY IT WAS FLAGGED

Bill repeals state preemption law that prohibited local firearm restrictions, enabling cities/counties to adopt their own stricter gun regulations—but the net effect of removing preemption actually allows for MORE local gun control, not less. However, the bill's title and intent frame this as loosening state-level prohibitions on local regulatory authority. This is technically pro-gun-rights by decentralizing control away from state preemption. Stance: +1 for expanding local autonomy over firearms policy.
