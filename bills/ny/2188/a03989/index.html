AN ACT to amend the executive law, in relation to requiring state agen-
cies to develop plans for new Americans
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Section 94-b of the executive law is amended by adding a
2 new subdivision 6 to read as follows:
3 6. State agency plans. Each state agency shall develop a plan for new
4 Americans that incorporates effective training and resources, ensures
5 culturally and linguistically competent and appropriate services, and
6 includes administrative practices that reach out to and reflect the
7 needs of immigrant populations and populations with limited English
8 proficiency. Each state agency shall consider the recommendations of the
9 office for new Americans when creating the agency's plans and shall
10 submit such plans to the governor for approval.
11 &#167; 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
