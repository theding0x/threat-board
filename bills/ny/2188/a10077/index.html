AN ACT to amend the public housing law, in relation to transferring the
village of Potsdam housing authority to the village of Canton housing
authority; and to repeal title 102 of article 13 of such law relating
to the village of Potsdam housing authority
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Title 102 of article 13 of the public housing law, as added
2 by chapter 533 of the laws of 1968, is REPEALED.
3 &#167; 2. Section 520 of the public housing law, as added by chapter 42 of
4 the laws of 1969, is amended to read as follows:
5 &#167; 520. Village of Canton housing authority. 1. A municipal housing
6 authority to be known as the village of Canton housing authority is
7 hereby created and established for the village of Canton in the county
8 of St. Lawrence for the accomplishment of any or all of the purposes
9 specified in article eighteen of the constitution of the state of New
10 York. It shall constitute a body corporate and politic, be perpetual in
11 duration and consist of five members. It shall have the powers and
12 duties now or hereafter conferred by this chapter upon municipal housing
13 authorities. It shall be organized in the manner prescribed by and
14 subject to the provisions of this chapter, and the authority, its
15 members, officers and employees and its operations and activities shall
16 in all respects be governed by the provisions of this chapter.
17 2. Notwithstanding any other provision of law, rule or regulation to
18 the contrary, the village of Potsdam housing authority, in its entirety,
19 shall be transferred to the village of Canton housing authority. All
20 powers and duties of the village of Potsdam housing authority pursuant
21 to section thirty-seven of this chapter or any other provision relating
22 to municipal housing authorities under article three of this chapter
23 shall be transferred to and assumed by the village of Canton housing
24 authority.
25 &#167; 3. Transfer of functions. All of the functions and powers possessed
26 by, and all of the obligations and duties of the village of Potsdam
27 housing authority are transferred and assigned to, assumed by and
28 devolved upon the village of Canton housing authority, in accordance
29 with and pursuant to the provisions of this act.
