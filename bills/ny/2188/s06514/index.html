AN ACT to amend the state finance law, in relation to RFPs for gun
violence reduction
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. The state finance law is amended by adding a new section
2 179-ff to read as follows:
3 &#167; 179-ff. Coordination plans; gun violence reduction. Notwithstanding
4 any provision of law, rule or regulation to the contrary, any state
5 agency that issues an RFP relating to gun violence reduction shall
6 establish a coordination plan including all awardees. Such coordination
7 plans shall be in a form as prescribed by the department of state. At a
8 minimum, such coordination plan shall help ensure complete coverage of
9 the area for which such RFP is issued and minimize competition between
10 awardees.
11 &#167; 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
