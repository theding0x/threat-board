The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. The labor law is amended by adding a new section 819-a to
2 read as follows:
3 &#167; 819-a. New York state public housing painters apprenticeship
4 program. 1. There is hereby established a New York state public housing
5 painters apprenticeship program. Such program will be administered by
6 the department, in coordination with the municipal housing authorities
7 established pursuant to article thirteen of the public housing law.
8 2. (a) Applications to participate in such apprenticeship program may
9 be submitted to the department in such form and manner as may be
10 prescribed by the commissioner.
11 (b) Eligibility for the New York state public housing painters appren-
12 ticeship program shall include, but not be limited to, the following
13 considerations:
14 (i) Participants in the program shall be limited to residents of a
15 municipal housing authority established pursuant to article thirteen of
16 the public housing law.
17 (ii) Applicants for the program shall not be deemed ineligible based
18 on criminal history.
19 3. Skills learned as part of the New York state public housing pain-
20 ters apprenticeship program shall include, but not be limited to:
21 (a) Workplace safety skills and procedures, including but not limited
22 to:
23 (i) use and maintenance of hand and power tools;
24 (ii) signaling, rigging, and hoisting;
