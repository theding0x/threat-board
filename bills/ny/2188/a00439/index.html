AN ACT to amend the general business law, in relation to removing deal-
ers of ammunition from certain provisions regarding the use of certain
merchant category-codes by payment card networks
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Subdivisions 1 and 2 of section 529-a of the general busi-
2 ness law, as added by a chapter of the laws of 2024 amending the general
3 business law relating to requiring payment card networks to use certain
4 merchant category-codes for firearm merchants, as proposed in legisla-
5 tive bills numbers S. 8479-A and A. 9862-A, are amended to read as
6 follows:
7 1. For the purposes of this section, the following terms shall have
8 the following meanings:
9 (a) [ "Dealer of ammunition" means any person who engages in the busi-
10 ness of purchasing, selling or keeping ammunition in this state.
11 (b) ] "Dealer of firearms" means a gunsmith or dealers in firearms in
12 this state licensed pursuant to section 400.00 of the penal law, for
13 which the highest sales value is, or is expected to be, from the
14 combined sale in New York of firearms, firearm accessories, or ammuni-
15 tion, as stated by the business to its merchant acquirer in the ordinary
16 course of business.
17 [ (c) ] (b) "Firearm" has the same meaning as that term is defined in
18 subdivision three of section 265.00 of the penal law.
19 2. (a) On or before November first, two thousand twenty-four, each
20 payment card network shall make the merchant category-code for firearms
21 and ammunition businesses established by the international organization
22 for standardization on September ninth, two thousand twenty-two, avail-
