AN ACT to amend the private housing finance law, in relation to estab-
lishing a New York Main Street development center
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. The private housing finance law is amended by adding a new
2 article 33 to read as follows:
3 ARTICLE XXXIII
4 NEW YORK MAIN STREET
5 DEVELOPMENT CENTER
6 Section 1300. Legislative intent.
7 1301. New York Main Street development center.
8 &#167; 1300. Legislative intent. The legislature hereby finds that with
9 affordable housing in increasingly short supply, the New York Main
10 Street development center will not only be the catalyst for community
11 renewal, but also address the critical need to provide affordable oppor-
12 tunities across the state. Main streets and downtown buildings could
13 serve as the primary location for new housing opportunities throughout
14 this state. Main streets and Downtown Business Districts, are uniquely
15 equipped to serve this function since these areas boast underutilized
16 water and sewer infrastructure, are the hubs of public transportation,
17 and are the centers of commerce.
18 &#167; 1301. New York Main Street development center. The division of hous-
19 ing and community renewal shall work in coordination with the department
20 of economic development, the department of state, the department of
21 transportation, and the office of parks, recreation and historic preser-
22 vation to establish a "New York Main Street development center" within
23 such division. The Main Street development center shall have as its
