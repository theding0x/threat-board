NY S08336 — Grants certain park rangers and building security of Erie county peace officer status to issue appearance tickets, simplified traffic informations, simplified parks informations and simplified environmental conservation informations; provides that such shall not be deemed to authorize such officer to carry, possess, repair or dispose of a firearm unless the appropriate license has been issued therefor.

The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Section 2.10 of the criminal procedure law is amended by
2 adding a new subdivision 88 to read as follows:
3 88. Park rangers of the Erie county department of parks, recreation
4 and forestry and building security of the Erie county department of
5 homeland security and emergency services, who shall be authorized to

7 parks informations and simplified environmental conservation informa-
8 tions; provided, however, that nothing in this subdivision shall be
9 deemed to authorize such officer to carry, possess, repair or dispose of
10 a firearm unless the appropriate license therefor has been issued pursu-
11 ant to section 400.00 of the penal law.
