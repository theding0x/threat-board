AN ACT to amend the administrative code of the city of New York, in
relation to requiring notification of residents who are potentially
affected by a serious health threat to their water system
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Section 17-194.1 of the administrative code of the city of
2 New York is amended by adding a new subdivision n to read as follows:
3 n. Notice requirements. In all cases where the results of the investi-
4 gation conducted pursuant to subdivision e of this section indicate a
5 serious health threat, the department shall notify all potentially
6 affected residents within 24 hours, including all adjacent buildings
7 that share the same underlying water system. The department shall
8 promulgate rules for compliance with the requirements of this subdivi-
9 sion, in accordance with applicable law.
10 &#167; 2. This act shall take effect on the ninetieth day after it shall
11 have become a law.
