AN ACT to create the 3D printed housing and infrastructure task force
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. 1. There is hereby created a task force to promote 3D
2 printed housing and infrastructure in New York state. The task force
3 shall consist of 11 members as follows: 5 members appointed by the
4 governor; 2 members appointed by the temporary president of the senate;
5 2 members appointed by the speaker of the assembly; and 2 members
6 appointed by the mayor of the city of New York. One representative shall
7 be from a building trades union; one representative shall be from a
8 business engaged in industrial 3D printing; one representative shall be
9 an expert on design who is a licensed architect; one representative
10 shall be a civil engineer who is a licensed professional engineer; one
11 representative shall be a certified urban planner; one representative
12 shall be a real estate developer; one representative shall be a repre-
13 sentative of the division of homes and community renewal; and one
14 representative shall be a member of the department of state division of
15 building standards and codes.
16 2. The task force shall develop recommendations on how the state can
17 promote large scale construction of 3D printed homes and infrastructure
18 with respect to legislation, regulatory environment, government initi-
19 atives and inducements.
20 3. The task force shall issue a report to the governor and the legis-
21 lature, on or before July 1, 2027 on the promotion of 3D printed housing
22 and infrastructure in New York state, including state and local initi-
23 atives, regulations, and incentives that will promote the manufacturing
24 of cost-effective, sustainable, and safe 3D printed housing and infras-
25 tructure while expediting project delivery and creating and preserving
