AN ACT to amend the agriculture and markets law, in relation to requir-
ing the establishment of a plant-based agriculture grant program
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. The agriculture and markets law is amended by adding a new
2 section 16-c to read as follows:
3 &#167; 16-c. Plant-based agriculture grant program. 1. For purposes of this
4 section, the following definitions apply:
5 (a) "Expert assistance" means assistance from an agricultural scien-
6 tist, climatologist, pedologist, horticulturist, hydrologist, or agro-
7 nomist for assessment, design, planning, and best management practices
8 of a land-use transition to plant-based agriculture.
9 (b) "Feed crop" means a crop that is grown for livestock consumption.
10 (c) "Livestock" means poultry, cattle, dairy cows, sheep, swine, goat,
11 or fish.
12 (d) "Plant-based agriculture" means any farming that uses crops for
13 growing inputs for plant-based products and does not include livestock
14 farming, dairy, or any crop production for livestock feed.
15 (e) "Program" means the plant-based agriculture grant program.
16 (f) "Technical assistance" means outreach, education, expert assist-
17 ance, legal support for contractual barriers, project planning, project
18 design, grant application assistance, buyer expertise and packaging
19 assistance, project implementation, or project reporting assistance
20 provided to a farmer to improve their successful participation in the
21 program.
22 (g) "Technical assistance provider" means organizations with demon-
23 strated technical expertise in designing and implementing agricultural
24 management practices.
