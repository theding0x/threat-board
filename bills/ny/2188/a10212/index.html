AN ACT to amend the local finance law, in relation to the period of

infrastructure
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Short title. This act shall be known and may be cited as
2 the "municipal broadband financing modernization improvement act of
3 2026".
4 &#167; 2. Subdivision 109 of paragraph a of section 11.00 of the local
5 finance law, as added by chapter 512 of the laws of 2023, is amended to
6 read as follows:
7 109. For acquisition, construction, or replacement of broadband and
8 related telecommunications infrastructure pursuant to section ninety-
9 nine-y of the general municipal law, [ ten ] thirty years.
10 &#167; 3. This act shall take effect immediately.
