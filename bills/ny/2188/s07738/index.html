AN ACT to amend the social services law, in relation to authorizing
domestic violence shelters to be reimbursed for housing a single indi-
vidual in a room intended for double occupancy
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Section 131-u of the social services law is amended by
2 adding two new subdivisions 3 and 4 to read as follows:
3 3. Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, a resi-
4 dential program for victims of domestic violence, as defined in section
5 four hundred fifty-nine-a of this chapter, which operates an emergency
6 shelter with at least ten beds and provides temporary housing assistance
7 to a single individual who meets such program's necessary eligibility
8 requirements, shall be reimbursed for the payment differential, if any,
9 for housing a single individual in a room intended for double occupancy,
10 if no other room intended for single occupancy is available within the
11 facility in accordance with the following:
12 (a) for the fiscal year beginning April first, two thousand twenty-
13 seven the reimbursement shall be fifty percent of any cost differential;
14 (b) for the fiscal year beginning April first, two thousand twenty-
15 eight the reimbursement shall be seventy-five percent of any cost
16 differential; and
17 (c) for the fiscal year beginning April first, two thousand twenty-
18 nine the reimbursement shall be one hundred percent of any cost differ-
19 ential.
