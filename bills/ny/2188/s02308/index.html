AN ACT to amend the transportation law, in relation to motorcycle aware-
ness on state highways
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Section 14 of the transportation law is amended by adding a
2 new subdivision 37 to read as follows:
3 37. To install signs on all state highways maintained by the depart-
4 ment in an effort to raise motorcycle awareness. Such signs shall clear-
5 ly state "Watch for Motorcycles". The design, place and manner of
6 installation of such signs shall conform to the manual on uniform traf-
7 fic control devices adopted by the commissioner.
8 &#167; 2. This act shall take effect on the first of April next succeeding
9 the date upon which it shall have become a law. Effective immediately,
10 the addition, amendment and/or repeal of any rule or regulation neces-
11 sary for the implementation of this act on its effective date are
12 authorized to be made and completed on or before such effective date.
