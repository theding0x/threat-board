the state highway system to Trooper Arthur A. Reddy
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. The highway law is amended by adding a new section 344-s to
2 read as follows:
3 &#167; 344-s. Portion of the state highway system to be dedicated to Troop-
4 er Arthur A. Reddy. The bridge over the Southern State Parkway on Deer
5 Park Avenue between Parkdale Drive and Sylvan Road in the town of Baby-
6 lon, county of Suffolk, shall be dedicated to Trooper Arthur A. Reddy.
7 &#167; 2. The commissioner of transportation shall provide for the instal-
8 lation and maintenance of adequate signing of the state highway system
9 as dedicated pursuant to section one of this act, provided that such
10 signing shall state "Bridge Dedicated to Trooper Arthur A Reddy". Howev-
11 er, to avoid confusion and to limit any possible disruption of commerce,
12 the dedication called for pursuant to section one of this act shall be
13 one of ceremonial nature and the official name of such bridge shall not
14 be changed as a result of this act.
15 &#167; 3. This act shall take effect immediately.
