AN ACT to amend the public authorities law, in relation to eligibility
for paratransit services
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Section 1299-f of the public authorities law is amended by
2 adding a new subdivision 8 to read as follows:
3 8. Notwithstanding any provisions of law, or the terms of any
4 contract, where the authority offers paratransit service to provide
5 public transportation for eligible individuals with disabilities or
6 health conditions that prevent them from using the public buses and
7 railroad operated by the authority for some or all of their trips, such
8 paratransit service shall be made available at the same fares for indi-
9 viduals with disabilities or health conditions who are able to utilize
10 the public buses and railroad operated by the authority.
11 &#167; 2. This act shall take effect on the ninetieth day after it shall
12 have become a law.
