AN ACT to amend the New York city civil court act, in relation to estab-
lishing a subpart to hear housing matters related to cooperative and
condominium buildings
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Section 110 of the New York city civil court act is amended
2 by adding a new subdivision (r) to read as follows:
3 (r) A separate subpart of the housing part established by this section
4 shall be devoted to actions and proceedings involving cooperative and
5 condominium buildings for such matters which are within the jurisdiction
6 of the housing part.
7 &#167; 2. This act shall take effect on the one hundred eightieth day after
8 it shall have become a law.
