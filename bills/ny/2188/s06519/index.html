AN ACT directing the commissioner of housing and community renewal, in
consultation with the director of the budget and the commissioner of
taxation and finance, to conduct a study to examine the efficacy of
the creation of a statewide affordable housing agency
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. The commissioner of housing and community renewal, in
2 consultation with the director of the budget and the commissioner of
3 taxation and finance, shall conduct a study to examine the efficacy of
4 the creation of a statewide affordable housing agency whose sole mission
5 is to directly build affordable housing utilizing state funds and
6 employing residents of the state to accomplish such construction of
7 affordable housing.
8 &#167; 2. The commissioner of housing and community renewal shall prepare a
9 report which shall be submitted to the governor, the temporary president
10 of the senate, the speaker of the assembly, the minority leader of the
11 senate and the minority leader of the assembly, within one year after
12 the effective date of this act, containing findings and recommendations
13 for potential legislative and regulatory actions to create such a state-
14 wide affordable housing agency.
15 &#167; 3. This act shall take effect immediately.
