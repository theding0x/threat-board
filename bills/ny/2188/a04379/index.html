AN ACT to amend the real property actions and proceedings law and the
New York city civil court act, in relation to the establishment of an
escrow account
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Section 701 of the real property actions and proceedings
2 law is amended by adding a new subdivision 3 to read as follows:
3 3. Any court which holds a special proceeding to recover real property
4 shall establish an escrow account. In order to appear in any such court,
5 a tenant shall submit any owed rent into such escrow account.
6 &#167; 2. Section 769 of the real property actions and proceedings law is
7 amended by adding a new subdivision 3 to read as follows:
8 3. Each court which holds special proceedings pursuant to subdivision
9 one of this section shall establish an escrow account. In order to
10 appear in such court, a tenant shall submit any owed rent into such
11 escrow account.
12 &#167; 3. Section 791 of the real property actions and proceedings law is
13 amended by adding a new subdivision 3 to read as follows:
14 3. Each court which holds special proceedings pursuant to subdivision
15 one of this section shall establish an escrow account. In order to
16 appear in court, a tenant shall submit any owed rent into such escrow
17 account.
18 &#167; 4. Section 796-a of the real property actions and proceedings law is
19 amended by adding a new subdivision 5 to read as follows:
20 5. Each court which holds special proceedings pursuant to subdivision
21 one of this section shall establish an escrow account. In order to
22 appear in such court, a tenant shall submit any owed rent into such
23 escrow account.
