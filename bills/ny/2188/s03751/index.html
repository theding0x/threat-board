AN ACT to require educational institutions with an endowment of over ten
billion dollars to provide a certain percentage of affordable housing
units
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Notwithstanding any other provision of law or any rule or
2 regulation to the contrary, any educational institution which has an
3 endowment of over ten billion dollars, and which is located in a city
4 with a population of one million or more, shall incorporate thirty
5 percent affordable housing units, as defined by subparagraph (xv) of
6 paragraph (a) of subdivision 16 of section 421-a of the real property
7 tax law, in any property owned by such institution which provides any
8 form of housing.
9 &#167; 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
