AN ACT to amend the highway law, in relation to designating a portion of
the state highway system as the "CW2 Casey N. Frankoski Memorial
Bridge"
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. The highway law is amended by adding a new section 344-p to
2 read as follows:
3 &#167; 344-p. Portion of the state highway system to be designated as the
4 "CW2 Casey N. Frankoski Memorial Bridge". The bridge located on Washing-
5 ton Avenue, designated as bridge number 1092600, which crosses over
6 interstate ninety, in the city of Rensselaer, county of Rensselaer,
7 shall be designated and known as the "CW2 Casey N. Frankoski Memorial
8 Bridge".
9 &#167; 2. The commissioner of transportation shall provide for the instal-
10 lation and maintenance of adequate signing of the state highway system
11 as designated pursuant to section one of this act. However, to avoid
12 confusion and to limit any possible disruption of commerce, the desig-
13 nation called for pursuant to section one of this act shall be one of
14 ceremonial nature and the official name of such bridge shall not be
15 changed as a result of this act.
16 &#167; 3. This act shall take effect immediately.
