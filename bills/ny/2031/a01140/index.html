NY A01140 — Provides for the regulation of short-term rental units; authorizes short-term rental units; requires short-term rental hosts to register each unit with the division of housing and community renewal.

ANTI UNDER REVIEW SPONSORS: 1 D
INTROCMTEFLOORPASSEDLAW
WHY IT WAS FLAGGED

Bill explicitly prohibits short-term rental units in properties classified as affordable housing, rent-controlled, or rent-stabilized, which reduces conversion of affordable units to short-term rentals but may reduce overall housing supply and tenant protections by enabling displacement of long-term tenants.
