AN ACT to amend the education law, in relation to inclusive instruction
and instructional materials
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. The education law is amended by adding a new section 804-e
2 to read as follows:
3 &#167; 804-e. Task force on model curriculum or instruction of the poli-
4 tical, economic, and social contributions of the LGBTQIA community. 1.
5 There is hereby created a task force to study and make recommendations
6 on model curriculum or instruction of the political, economic, and
7 social contributions of lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, queer,
8 intersex, and asexual people, in an appropriate place in the curriculum
9 of middle school and high school students. The task force shall also
10 study and make recommendations on policies, procedures and best prac-
11 tices for the selection and adoption of inclusive instructional materi-
12 als for such model curriculum.
13 2. The task force shall be composed of eleven members to be appointed
14 as follows:
15 (a) two members shall be appointed by the governor;
16 (b) two members shall be appointed by the temporary president of the
17 senate;
18 (c) two members shall be appointed by the speaker of the assembly;
19 (d) one member shall be appointed by the minority leader of the
20 senate;
