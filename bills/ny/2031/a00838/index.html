AN ACT to create a task force to review the affordable housing lotteries
within the state; and providing for the repeal of such provisions upon
expiration thereof
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. (a) There is hereby created within the division of housing
2 and community renewal a temporary task force on affordable housing
3 lotteries which, pursuant to the provisions of this section, shall
4 review the affordable housing lotteries within New York state and deter-
5 mine the lotteries' effectiveness and recommend how the lotteries could
6 be improved.
7 (b) The task force shall utilize information collected from organiza-
8 tions, programs and agencies both in New York city and throughout the
9 state to make its recommendations.
10 (c)(i) The members of the task force shall include the commissioner of
11 the division of housing and community renewal or his or her designee; at
12 least two representatives of tenants who have used affordable housing
13 lotteries within New York state; at least two representatives from
14 affordable housing lotteries within New York state; at least one expert
15 on affordable housing lotteries; and any other relevant agency or
16 participant that is deemed appropriate. The commissioner of the division
17 of housing and community renewal or his or her designee shall be desig-
18 nated as the chairperson of such task force and shall select a vice-
19 chairperson and a secretary.
20 (ii) The members of the task force shall receive no compensation for
21 their services but shall be reimbursed for expenses actually and neces-
22 sarily incurred in the performance of their duties.
23 (d) No later than one year after the effective date of this section,
24 the task force shall provide a report to the temporary president of the
