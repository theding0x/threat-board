Introduced by M. of A. L. ROSENTHAL -- read once and referred to the

AN ACT to amend the public housing law, in relation to requiring the New
York city housing authority to provide adequate and reliable internet
service for all housing authority residents
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. The public housing law is amended by adding a new section
2 402-g to read as follows:
3 &#167; 402-g. NYCHA internet service. The New York city housing authority
4 (NYCHA) shall be required to provide adequate and reliable internet
5 service at all NYCHA residential properties which can be accessed at any
6 time by NYCHA residents.
7 &#167; 2. Subdivision 1 of section 402-e of the public housing law, as
8 amended by chapter 47 of the laws of 2020, is amended to read as
9 follows:
10 1. In order to ensure compliance and enforcement of the New York city
11 housing authority's duty to provide heat, water, electricity , internet
12 service and elevator service, the New York city housing authority shall
13 publish information regarding reported disruptions in such services, the
14 length of such disruptions and the steps taken to restore services.
15 Starting on May first, two thousand twenty, the New York city housing
16 authority shall publish such information on its website. For six months
17 after the original publication date, the New York city housing authority
18 shall retain such information on its website.
19 &#167; 3. This act shall take effect immediately.
