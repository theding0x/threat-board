NY S06056 — Relates to certain economic development programs; requires reporting on the use of fiber-optic technology in certain economic development grant programs; adds access to high-speed broadband internet service as consideration in certain competitive grant projects.

PRO UNDER REVIEW SPONSORS: 2 D
WHY IT WAS FLAGGED

Bill promotes high-speed broadband infrastructure (fiber-optic technology) as a consideration and priority in economic development grant programs, effectively increasing public infrastructure investment in telecommunications.
