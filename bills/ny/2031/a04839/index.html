AN ACT to amend the real property law, in relation to the timely cashing
of rent checks
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. The real property law is amended by adding a new section
2 235-j to read as follows:
3 &#167; 235-j. Timely cashing of rent check. 1. A landlord shall cash a rent
4 check provided by a tenant in his or her possession within thirty days
5 after the check is postmarked.
6 2. Any landlord who violates this section shall be subject to a civil
7 penalty of two hundred fifty dollars.
8 &#167; 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
