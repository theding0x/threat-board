AN ACT to amend the public service law, in relation to requiring gas
corporations and electric corporations in this state to file quarterly
reports with the legislature regarding infrastructure upgrades
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. The public service law is amended by adding a new section
2 73-b to read as follows:
3 &#167; 73-b. Infrastructure upgrades; quarterly reports to legislature. 1.
4 On or before October first, two thousand twenty-three and on or before
5 every January first, April first, July first and October first thereaft-
6 er, every gas corporation and electric corporation in this state shall
7 file a report with the legislature setting forth:
8 (a) the scope and nature of all upgrades to the infrastructure of such
9 corporation completed during the previous quarterly period;
10 (b) the status of any ongoing upgrades to the infrastructure of such
11 corporation and the scope and nature of such upgrade; and
12 (c) the status regarding the planning and development of any future
13 upgrades to the infrastructure of such corporation and the scope and
14 nature of such upgrade.
15 2. Such report shall set forth in detail infrastructure upgrades with
16 respect to infrastructure hardening, storm resilience and climate
17 change.
18 &#167; 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
