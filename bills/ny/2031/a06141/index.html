NY · 2023-2024 GENERAL ASSEMBLY · INTRODUCED (04-03)

NY A06141 — Establishes the crimes of carjacking in first, second and third degrees, as the stealing of a motor vehicle from a person or presence of another person through the use or threatened use of force; increases penalties for causing injury to such victim, displaying a real or fake gun or using such a weapon; provides that all carjackings are violent felony offenses.

22 person.
23 Carjacking in the third degree is a class D felony.
