NY A06048 — Establishes a private right of action for tenants alleging a failure to keep and maintain self-closing doors; allows for a court to award compensatory damages, reasonable attorney's fees and court costs and issue an order to the owner of the building to remedy any issue with such doors.

WHY IT WAS FLAGGED

The bill enhances tenant protections by creating a private right of action for housing code violations (self-closing doors), enabling tenants to recover damages and attorney fees while ensuring remediation—protections that support housing habitability and affordability for vulnerable renters.
