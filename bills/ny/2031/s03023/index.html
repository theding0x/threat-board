AN ACT to amend the public housing law and the administrative code of
the city of New York, in relation to establishing an on site super-
intendent or maintenance supervisor at city of New York Housing
Authority facilities, including janitorial services to maintain work-
able locks on doors; and to repeal section 27-2056 of the administra-
tive code of the city of New York, relating to the exemption to the
housing authority under such code
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Subdivision 5-a of section 402 of the public housing law is
2 amended by adding a new paragraph g to read as follows:
3 g. Notwithstanding any provisions to the contrary contained in this
4 chapter, or any general, special or local law, the city of New York
5 housing authority shall have the power to provide and maintain on site
6 superintendents and maintenance supervisors at all city of New York
7 housing authority facilities. Such site superintendent or maintenance
8 supervisor shall have the duty, in and about housing facilities, to
9 preserve the cleanliness and functionality of the facility and other
10 requirements the authority deems necessary. Appointments to site super-
11 intendent or maintenance supervisor shall be made in accordance with
12 applicable provisions of the civil service law and only persons who have
13 never been convicted of a felony, and who are citizens of the United
14 States shall be appointed site superintendent or maintenance supervisor.
