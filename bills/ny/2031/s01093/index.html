NY · 2023-2024 GENERAL ASSEMBLY · INTRODUCED (01-10)

NY S01093 — Enacts the "Omnibus Prevailing Wage Enforcement Act"; creates the office of the prevailing wage fraud inspector general for increased vigilance in the effort to achieve prevailing wage enforcement; allows public authorities to require that successful bidders for public works jobs participate in a state-sponsored apprenticeship program; makes related provisions.

NEUTRAL UNDER REVIEW SPONSORS: 1 D
INTROCMTEFLOORPASSEDLAW
WHY IT WAS FLAGGED

Bill focuses on enforcement mechanisms, apprenticeship requirements, and prevailing wage provisions for public works contracts, but does not directly increase or decrease funding levels—it addresses how existing public works funding is allocated and monitored.
