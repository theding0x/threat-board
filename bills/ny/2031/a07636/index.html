NY A07636 — Establishes the homeowner protection program; provides that the department of law shall establish the homeowner protection program to ensure the availability of free housing counseling and legal services to homeowners for the purposes of mitigating threats to homeownership; provides that the department of law shall provide grants to eligible not-for-profit housing counseling organizations and legal services organizations to provide services under the program.

WHY IT WAS FLAGGED

The bill establishes a program providing free housing counseling and legal services to homeowners facing threats to homeownership, including foreclosure prevention and preservation—directly protecting homeowners' ability to maintain housing, which supports housing stability and protections.
