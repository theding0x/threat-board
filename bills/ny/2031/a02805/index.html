NY A02805 — Enacts the residential structure fire prevention act; provides a tax credit to homeowners who remove cock loft fire hazards in their homes; provides an insurance discount for the installation of smoke detecting alarm devices in cock lofts; provides that the repair of cock lofts for the purpose of fire prevention and safety shall be qualifying expenditures under state housing programs.

WHY IT WAS FLAGGED

Bill includes cock loft repairs as qualifying expenditures under state housing programs, which can facilitate housing improvements and affordability initiatives, though the primary focus is fire safety rather than directly increasing affordable housing supply or tenant protections.
