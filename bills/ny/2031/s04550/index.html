NY · 2023-2024 GENERAL ASSEMBLY · PASSED (12-21)

NY S04550 — Enacts the "schools impacted by gross highways (SIGH) act"; prohibits the commissioner of education from approving the plans for the erection of any new schoolhouse within five hundred feet of a controlled-access highway unless the commissioner of education determines that space limitations are so severe that there is no other site to erect such new schoolhouse, and there are adequate engineering controls to address air quality; makes related provisions.

ANTI UNDER REVIEW SPONSORS: 7 D · 1 R
INTROCMTEFLOORPASSEDLAW
WHY IT WAS FLAGGED

The bill restricts where new schoolhouses can be built (within 500 feet of highways), which constrains public infrastructure development and may increase costs by limiting site options, effectively reducing the efficiency of public works funding for school construction.
