NY · 2023-2024 GENERAL ASSEMBLY · INTRODUCED (01-17)

NY A01361 — Defines community significant projects as a business creating or retaining current jobs as determined by the commissioner, with particular emphasis on employment and/or training of current public housing residents; currently located or to be located in existing leased space of a building in a public housing development in the state; and which makes significant qualified capital investments to start a business, or improve services and working conditions for an existing business, when located in such public housing space and including such projects in the excelsior jobs program.

WHY IT WAS FLAGGED

Bill promotes business development in public housing developments with emphasis on employment of public housing residents, improved services/working conditions, and safeguards against displacement of residents or existing services, which protects public housing affordability and resident welfare.
