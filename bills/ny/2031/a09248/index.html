AN ACT to amend the transportation law and the public authorities law,
in relation to requiring the installation of climb deterrent fencing
on certain bridges
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Section 14 of the transportation law is amended by adding a
2 new subdivision 16-a to read as follows:
3 16-a. The commissioner shall ensure that climb deterrent fencing is
4 installed on all bridges under the jurisdiction of the New York State
5 Bridge Authority. For purposes of this section, "climb deterrent fenc-
6 ing" shall mean fencing designed for deterrence of climbing and prevent-
7 ing grip and footholds, equipped with certain features including but not
8 limited to welded wire mesh panels, tightly spaced wires, and spike
9 collars.
10 &#167; 2. Section 530 of the public authorities law, as amended by chapter
11 420 of the laws of 1968, is amended to read as follows:
12 &#167; 530. Construction contracts. The authority shall construct the
13 bridges pursuant to a contract or contracts. The authority shall
14 construct the bridges pursuant to a contract or contracts. The authority
15 shall install climb deterrent fencing as defined in subdivision
16 sixteen-a of section fourteen of the transportation law on all bridges
17 under its jurisdiction. Such contracts shall be let under the super-
18 vision of the commissioner and shall be subject to [ his ] the approval of
19 such commissioner . Such contracts shall be let by the authority in the
20 manner, so far as practicable, provided in section thirty-eight of the
21 highway law, but failure to comply with such section shall not invali-
22 date such contracts. Requisitions for the payments to be made pursuant
23 to such contracts shall be approved by the commissioner. The work under
24 such contracts shall be subject to the supervision and approval of the
25 commissioner.
EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets
[ ] is old law to be omitted.
