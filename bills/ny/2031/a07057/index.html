NY · 2023-2024 GENERAL ASSEMBLY · INTRODUCED (05-10)

NY A07057 — Enacts the "tenants organizing act"; allows tenants' groups, committees or other tenants' organizations to invite outside guests or visitors including elected officials, services providers, housing lawyers, etc. without being required to pay fees; defines "tenants' organization"; provides for activities that tenants' organizations can take part in; provides for the manner in how a tenants' organization is established.

PRO UNDER REVIEW SPONSORS: 2 D
INTROCMTEFLOORPASSEDLAW
WHY IT WAS FLAGGED

Bill strengthens tenant protections by enabling tenant organizing, prohibiting landlord interference, guaranteeing meeting rights, and requiring good-faith landlord-tenant conferencing on housing conditions.
