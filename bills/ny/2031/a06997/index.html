NY · 2023-2024 GENERAL ASSEMBLY · INTRODUCED (05-10)

NY A06997 — Standardizes the language across the relevant court acts to allow tenants statewide to sue in the county where the real property at issue is located in matters relating to a tenancy or lease; requires the Office of Court Administration to develop necessary forms, procedures and deliver a report.

PRO UNDER REVIEW SPONSORS: 5 D
INTROCMTEFLOORPASSEDLAW
WHY IT WAS FLAGGED

Bill expands tenant protections by allowing tenants to sue in the county where rental property is located rather than where the landlord resides, reducing litigation barriers for tenant claims related to tenancy disputes.
