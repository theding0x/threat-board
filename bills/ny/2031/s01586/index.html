NY S01586 — Enacts shoreline resiliency infrastructure regulations providing for preventive measures that could be taken to mitigate the impact of future flooding and provides tax credits for twenty-five percent of the costs of such projects.

WHY IT WAS FLAGGED

Bill increases infrastructure funding through tax credits (25% of costs, up to $10k/year) for shoreline resiliency infrastructure projects, which are public works designed to mitigate flooding impacts. However, the mechanism is indirect (tax credits to private entities) rather than direct public funding appropriation.
