AN ACT to amend the transportation law and the public authorities law,
in relation to requiring the installation of climb deterrent fencing
on certain bridges
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Section 14 of the transportation law is amended by adding a
2 new subdivision 16-a to read as follows:
3 16-a. The commissioner shall ensure that climb deterrent fencing is
4 installed on all bridges under the jurisdiction of the New York State
5 Bridge Authority. For purposes of this section, "climb deterrent fenc-
6 ing" shall mean fencing designed for deterrence of climbing and prevent-
7 ing grip and footholds, equipped with certain features including but not
8 limited to welded wire mesh panels, tightly spaced wires, and spike
9 collars.

17 under its jurisdiction. Such contracts shall be let under the super-
18 vision of the commissioner and shall be subject to [ his ] the approval of
19 such commissioner . Such contracts shall be let by the authority in the
20 manner, so far as practicable, provided in section thirty-eight of the
