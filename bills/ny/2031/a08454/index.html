NY · 2023-2024 GENERAL ASSEMBLY · INTRODUCED (12-29)

NY A08454 — Requires firearms, rifles and shotguns to be manufactured or modified to be permanently incompatible with a rapid-fire modification device; requires manufacturers of such weapons to make such modifications to existing weapons at no cost to owners and dealers; gives owners one year to obtain compliance.

ANTI SPONSORS: 2 D

WHY IT WAS FLAGGED

Bill substantially tightens firearm restrictions by mandating all firearms be manufactured to be incompatible with rapid-fire devices, criminalizing manufacture/transport/disposal of non-compliant firearms as felonies, and requiring manufacturers to modify existing weapons at no cost—all clear expansions of gun control.
