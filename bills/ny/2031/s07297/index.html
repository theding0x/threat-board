NY S07297 — Establishes the homeowner protection program; provides that the department of law shall establish the homeowner protection program to ensure the availability of free housing counseling and legal services to homeowners for the purposes of mitigating threats to homeownership; provides that the department of law shall provide grants to eligible not-for-profit housing counseling organizations and legal services organizations to provide services under the program.

PRO UNDER REVIEW SPONSORS: 9 D
WHY IT WAS FLAGGED

The bill establishes a homeowner protection program providing free housing counseling and legal services to prevent foreclosures, displacement, and loss of homeownership, which protects homeowners from losing their housing and preserves homeownership stability in vulnerable communities including communities of color.
