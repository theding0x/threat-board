NY A10047 — Establishes a senior housing task force for the purposes of conducting a state-wide assessment of senior housing needs taking into consideration the various geographical areas and different needs of the seniors in those areas and resources available in those different areas.

WHY IT WAS FLAGGED

The bill establishes a task force to assess senior housing needs and develop a state plan, which relates to affordable housing policy, but it is preparatory in nature—creating assessment mechanisms rather than directly increasing supply or providing tenant protections.
