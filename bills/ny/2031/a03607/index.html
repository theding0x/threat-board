NY · 2023-2024 GENERAL ASSEMBLY · INTRODUCED (02-03)

NY A03607 — Relates to the brownfield redevelopment tax credit; provides that in the event that the qualified site is owned by, or was acquired by a taxpayer from, a municipality and such municipality is a volunteer under the brownfield site clean-up agreement with respect to such qualified site, then the amount of the credit shall be reduced; defines "infrastructure work"; makes related amendments.

ANTI UNDER REVIEW SPONSORS: 1 D
INTROCMTEFLOORPASSEDLAW
WHY IT WAS FLAGGED

The bill reduces brownfield redevelopment tax credits when municipalities perform infrastructure work, site preparation, or public improvements, effectively decreasing public funding available for infrastructure development by redirecting costs to municipalities rather than allowing full taxpayer credits.
