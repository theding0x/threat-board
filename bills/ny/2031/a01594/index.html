NY · 2023-2024 GENERAL ASSEMBLY · INTRODUCED (01-17)

NY A01594 — Requires the departments of health and mental hygiene to create a pamphlet of mental health resources for those who have suffered a pregnancy loss, including, but not limited to, a miscarriage, stillbirth or abortion; requires providers to give patients an adequate supply of menstrual products upon discharge to help with their aftercare.

NEUTRAL UNDER REVIEW SPONSORS: 1 D

WHY IT WAS FLAGGED

Bill treats abortion as one among multiple pregnancy loss types (miscarriage, stillbirth, abortion) requiring mental health support; does not expand/protect abortion access nor restrict/ban it, making it neutral on the abortion lens.
