NY · 2023-2024 GENERAL ASSEMBLY · INTRODUCED (04-11)

NY A06466 — Provides that the contractor who performed the construction work which results in an illegal conversion of a housing unit shall be held liable for monetary penalties related to such illegal conversion, rather than the residents; provides that the owner of the building shall be held liable if he or she knew or should reasonably have known that the construction was being done without prior approval by the department of buildings and without the required permits.

WHY IT WAS FLAGGED

The bill protects residents from bearing financial penalties for illegal housing conversions, reducing their liability and indirectly supporting tenant protections by preventing cost-shifting to residents who may lack resources.
