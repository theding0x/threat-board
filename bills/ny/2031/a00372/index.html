NY · 2023-2024 GENERAL ASSEMBLY · INTRODUCED (01-06)

NY A00372 — Establishes the lesbian, gay, bisexual and transgender, and people living with HIV long-term care facility residents' bill of rights; prohibits a long-term care facility or facility staff from discriminating against any resident on the basis of such resident's actual or perceived sexual orientation, gender identity or expression, or HIV status.

PRO SPONSORS: 13 D

WHY IT WAS FLAGGED

Bill explicitly establishes comprehensive protections for LGBTQ individuals and people with HIV in long-term care facilities, prohibiting discrimination, mandating use of preferred pronouns/names, allowing gender-identity-aligned room assignments, and requiring cultural competency training.
