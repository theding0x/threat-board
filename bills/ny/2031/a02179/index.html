NY A02179 — Defines "probable aggregate annual income" for purposes of determining eligibility for limited profit and limited dividend housing companies, as the annual net income after federal, state and municipal income taxes are deducted from gross income of the chief wage earner.

WHY IT WAS FLAGGED

Bill clarifies income calculation methods for determining eligibility for limited profit housing programs, potentially broadening eligibility by including all household income after taxes rather than just chief wage earner, which could increase access to affordable housing for eligible families.
