AN ACT to amend the executive law, in relation to requiring the division
of criminal justice services to establish and maintain a database of
information relating to the sale or use of microstamped guns in the
state and to promulgate regulations regarding the provision of infor-
mation pertaining to the sale, delivery or use of such guns within the
state
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Section 837 of the executive law is amended by adding a new
2 subdivision 24 to read as follows:
3 24. (a) The department shall establish, maintain and update on a quar-
4 terly basis an electronic database of information relating to the manu-
5 facture, sale, offer for sale, exchange, giving, transfer, delivery or
6 use of microstamped guns in the state. Such database shall be imple-
7 mented within one hundred twenty days of the effective date of a New
8 York state or federal law, rule or regulation mandating the microstamp-
9 ing of any type of gun.
10 (b) As used in this subdivision, "microstamped gun" means a gun that
11 has been manufactured, equipped with or modified to contain a component
12 or mechanism that is intended to produce a unique alpha-numeric or
13 geometric code on at least one location on each expended cartridge case
14 fired from the gun that identifies the make, model, and serial number of
15 the gun.
16 (c) Such database shall contain, at a minimum and as applicable:
17 (i) the make, model and serial number of the gun;
18 (ii) the identity and address of the dealer in firearms licensed under
19 section 400.00 of the penal law conveying the gun;
20 (iii) the identity and address of the manufacturer delivering or caus-
21 ing the delivery of the gun to a person or entity within the state;
