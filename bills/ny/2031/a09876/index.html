NY · 2023-2024 GENERAL ASSEMBLY · INTRODUCED (04-24)

NY A09876 — Establishes the shovel-ready housing program under which certain municipalities, at their option, may pre-authorize parcels of property for the construction of housing; establishes a shovel-ready community tax rebate for taxpayers residing in municipalities that opt-in to the shovel-ready housing program; provides for annual apportionment to municipalities that have certified participation in the shovel-ready housing program.

WHY IT WAS FLAGGED

Bill establishes a shovel-ready housing program to pre-authorize housing construction and provides tax incentives/municipal grants to encourage housing development, directly aimed at increasing housing supply; however, the bill does not explicitly mandate affordability requirements or tenant protections, limiting confidence.
