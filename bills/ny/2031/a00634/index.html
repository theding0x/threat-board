NY · 2023-2024 GENERAL ASSEMBLY · INTRODUCED (01-11)

NY A00634 — Proscribes conduct constituting a bias related crime, meaning for such purposes, the commission of a designated act that demonstrates a prejudice based on the race, color, religion, national origin, age, ethnicity, disability, gender or sexual orientation of the victim; denies the granting of youthful offender status for any offender committing such a crime.

WHY IT WAS FLAGGED
