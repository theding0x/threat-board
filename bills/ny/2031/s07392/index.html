AN ACT to amend the general business law, in relation to the dangers to
safety and health and the creation of a public nuisance caused by the
sale, manufacturing, distribution, importing, and marketing of
firearms
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Subdivision 6 of section 898-a of the general business law,
2 as added by chapter 237 of the laws of 2021, is amended to read as
3 follows:
4 6. "Qualified product" shall [ have the same meaning as defined in 15
5 U.S.C. section 7903(4) ] mean a firearm, as defined in subparagraph (A)
6 or (B) of 18 U.S.C. section 921(a)(3), including any antique firearm,
7 as defined in 18 U.S.C. section 921(a)(16), or ammunition, as defined
8 in 18 U.S.C. section 921(a)(17)(A), or a component part of a firearm or
9 ammunition .
10 &#167; 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
