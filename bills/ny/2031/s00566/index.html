8 &#167; 1290. Statement of legislative findings and purpose. The legislature
9 finds and declares that climate change is having a significant impact
10 upon our environment, health, and safety and it is necessary to imple-
11 ment programs and policies that immediately reduce greenhouse gas emis-
12 sions. The legislature further finds that residential housing is a
13 source for such emissions and we can only address climate change in a
14 meaningful manner if we change our practices and behaviors as they
15 relate to the construction of and improvements to such housing, such as
16 the use of environmentally-sensitive design and the construction prac-
17 tices and materials; the installation of energy efficient products; and
18 access to renewable energy sources. Establishing a new program to
19 encourage and publicly subsidize more environmentally-friendly practices
20 and materials will reduce greenhouse gas emissions from multi-unit hous-
21 ing throughout New York state and help address climate change for future
22 generations.
