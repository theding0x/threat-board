NY A01668 — Provides that any vehicle which: has been issued a permit pursuant to subdivision 15 of section 385 of the vehicle and traffic law; is transporting road salt; and does not have a gross vehicle weight rating in excess of one hundred seven thousand pounds, shall be authorized to operate on all public highways and bridges at any time between the months of October and April.

WHY IT WAS FLAGGED

Bill facilitates road salt transportation by permitting heavier vehicles on highways during winter months, reducing operational constraints and costs for road maintenance/de-icing operations—a core public works activity.
