NY A07148 — Relates to the prosecution of certain juveniles between the ages of 16 and 18 charged with certain felony offenses, including possession of a firearm; relates to the transfer of such cases to family court.

ANTI UNDER REVIEW SPONSORS: 1 D
INTROCMTEFLOORPASSEDLAW
WHY IT WAS FLAGGED

Bill restricts prosecution of juveniles charged with firearm possession by creating presumption for transfer to family court, but simultaneously makes it harder to transfer cases involving juveniles who possessed firearms (excepting such cases from automatic removal), effectively tightening restrictions on juvenile firearm offense prosecutions.
