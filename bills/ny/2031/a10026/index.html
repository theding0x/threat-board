AN ACT to amend the public housing law, in relation to enacting the
"housing is a human right act"
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Short title. This act shall be known and may be cited as
2 the "housing is a human right act".
3 &#167; 2. Section 14 of the public housing law is amended by adding a new
4 subdivision 8 to read as follows:
5 8. (a) The commissioner shall appoint an individual, otherwise known
6 as the New York state affordable housing czar, who shall create a
7 centralized office within the division of housing to advocate for and
8 oversee all of the state's housing initiatives, work with key stakehold-
9 ers, tenants, landlords, and developers to create an integrated state
10 housing plan to generate affordable and subsidized housing, ensure that
11 existing housing is utilized to prevent homelessness, and ensure state
12 residents have access to the state's growing number of affordable apart-
13 ments and homes. Responsibilities of the New York state affordable hous-
14 ing czar shall include, but not be limited to:
15 (i) making state residents aware of the range of affordable housing
16 opportunities;
17 (ii) creating, maintaining, and publicizing a central website where
18 state residents can apply for affordable housing units, or be directed
19 to where to apply for such units;
20 (iii) serving as an ombudsman for individuals seeking public or subsi-
21 dized housing;
22 (iv) monitoring developer's compliance with state housing mandates;
23 (v) ensuring compliance with all state affordable housing mandates and
24 covenants;
25 (vi) overseeing the development and implementation of a five-year
26 affordable housing compliance and management plan; and
27 (vii) ensuring state residents and community involvement in affordable
28 housing policy development.
