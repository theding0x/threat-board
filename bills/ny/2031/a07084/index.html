NY · 2023-2024 GENERAL ASSEMBLY · INTRODUCED (05-10)

NY A07084 — Requires search engines such as Google, Yahoo or Bing to include a notice to users when searching limited services pregnancy center listings that such limited services pregnancy centers provide limited medical and abortion care and may not have medical professionals onsite; requires social media networks such as Facebook, YouTube, Twitter, Instagram, Snapchat, Tumblr and Flickr to include such consumer notice on any posts or advertisements by such centers.

PRO UNDER REVIEW SPONSORS: 10 D
INTROCMTEFLOORPASSEDLAW
WHY IT WAS FLAGGED

Bill requires disclosure notices on limited services pregnancy centers to inform consumers that these facilities do not provide comprehensive reproductive care, which indirectly supports abortion access by preventing misleading information that could deter people from seeking legitimate abortion services.
