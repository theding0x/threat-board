NY · 2023-2024 GENERAL ASSEMBLY · INTRODUCED (01-11)

NY A00709 — Enacts the "gender identity respect, dignity and safety act"; relates to the treatment and placement of incarcerated individuals based upon gender identity; requires that incarcerated individuals in state and local correctional facilities who have a gender identity different from their assigned sex at birth be addressed and have access to commissary items, clothing and other materials that are consistent with the person's gender identity; establishes that incarcerated individuals shall be presumptively placed in a correctional facility with persons of the gender that most closely aligns with such person's self-attested gender identity unless the person opts out of such placement.

WHY IT WAS FLAGGED

Bill comprehensively expands LGBTQ rights for incarcerated transgender and gender nonconforming individuals by mandating respectful treatment using affirmed names/pronouns, access to gender-affirming materials and healthcare, presumptive placement in facilities matching gender identity, and explicit non-discrimination protections.
