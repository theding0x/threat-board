NY · 2023-2024 GENERAL ASSEMBLY · INTRODUCED (02-01)

NY A02929 — Makes it a class B violent felony to fire a gun into a crowded space with the intent to harm or absent the intent to harm when such an act creates a great risk of death to one or more people within the crowded space; establishes the crime of criminal use of weapons; firing into a crowded space.
