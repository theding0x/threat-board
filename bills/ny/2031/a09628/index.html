WHY IT WAS FLAGGED

The bill protects affordable housing tenants and properties by requiring out-of-state owners to maintain escrow accounts for essential services (utilities, taxes, maintenance, fire services), ensuring funds are available for proper upkeep and reducing risk of neglect or service disruptions that could harm affordable housing quality and tenant welfare.
