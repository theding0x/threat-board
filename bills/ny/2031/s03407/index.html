NY · 2023-2024 GENERAL ASSEMBLY · INTRODUCED (01-31)

NY S03407 — Provides for the regulation of short-term rental units; authorizes short-term rental units; requires short-term rental hosts to register each unit with the division of housing and community renewal.

ANTI UNDER REVIEW SPONSORS: 4 D
INTROCMTEFLOORPASSEDLAW
WHY IT WAS FLAGGED

Bill explicitly excludes rent-controlled, rent-stabilized, and affordable housing units from short-term rental conversion (Section 289.1.e), which protects the affordable housing stock but the overall regulatory framework enabling short-term rentals could increase conversion pressure on other housing units and reduce long-term rental supply.
