The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Section 20 of the social services law is amended by adding
2 a new subdivision 9 to read as follows:
3 9. (a) The department shall establish a centralized homelessness
4 crisis response data system through which state agencies, local govern-
5 mental entities, including law enforcement agencies, court systems,
6 school districts, and emergency service providers, and other relevant

16 those challenges; and
17 (ii) enter into data sharing agreements as necessary for the exchange
18 of information relating to individuals experiencing chronic homelessness
19 through the centralized homelessness crisis response data system.
20 (c) The centralized homelessness crisis response data system estab-
21 lished under this section shall:
22 (i) to the extent permitted by a data sharing agreement, collect data
23 from other homeless crisis response systems maintained or operated by
24 other state entities; and
