AN ACT to amend the general business law, in relation to requiring fire
insurance for any building where bicycles with electric assist are
repaired, sold, rented, or stored for repair, sale or rental
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. The general business law is amended by adding a new section
2 391-w to read as follows:
3 &#167; 391-w. Repair, sale, or rental of bicycles with electric assist;
4 fire insurance. 1. On and after January first, two thousand twenty-four,
5 any owner of a building where bicycles with electric assist are
6 repaired, sold, rented, or stored for repair, sale or rental shall:
7 (a) maintain fire insurance coverage of at least one million dollars
8 and an additional one hundred thousand dollars per tenant if tenants
9 reside in such building; and
10 (b) maintain a certificate of insurance demonstrating compliance with
11 this section.
12 2. Whenever a fire-related emergency or other fire-related incident
13 occurs in a building where bicycles with electric assist are repaired,
14 sold or rented, or stored for repair, sale or rental, no bicycles with
15 electric assist, including batteries or electrical systems for bicycles
16 with electric assist, shall be stored on the premises for any purpose
17 until the premises have been inspected and determined to be in compli-
18 ance with all relevant laws and regulations by an appropriate and quali-
19 fied inspector as determined by the local government.
20 3. For the purposes of this section, "bicycle with electric assist"
21 shall have the same meaning as section one hundred two-c of the vehicle
