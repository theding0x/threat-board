NY · 2023-2024 GENERAL ASSEMBLY · INTRODUCED (02-03)

NY S04120 — Provides for a 75-25 percent split in the New York city transit authority's operating surplus between the authority and the Metropolitan transit authority where previously the split was 50-50.

PRO UNDER REVIEW SPONSORS: 1 R

WHY IT WAS FLAGGED

The bill increases funding available to the Metropolitan Transportation Authority (from 50% to 75% of operating surplus) which is a public transportation/infrastructure authority responsible for rail and transit operations serving the NYC metropolitan area.
