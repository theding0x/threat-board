NY · 2023-2024 GENERAL ASSEMBLY · INTRODUCED (01-11)

NY S01332 — Amends the tenant dignity and safe housing act relating to special proceedings for judgment directing repairs of conditions and other relief in residential real property to limit jurisdiction; provides that proceedings may be maintained against any respondent with a legal obligation to maintain the property.

PRO UNDER REVIEW SPONSORS: 1 D
INTROCMTEFLOORPASSEDLAW
WHY IT WAS FLAGGED

Bill strengthens tenant protections by broadening the definition of parties who can be sued for housing condition violations (anyone with legal obligation to maintain), expanding court jurisdiction, and improving access to repair remedies—core tenant protection mechanisms.
