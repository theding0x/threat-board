The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Section 2.10 of the criminal procedure law is amended by
2 adding a new subdivision 14 to read as follows:
3 14. Trained personnel of the Physical Security Organization of a
4 nuclear facility licensed by the United States Nuclear Regulatory
5 Commission responsible for maintaining safeguards and protection pursu-
6 ant to the licensee's security plan when acting pursuant to their
7 special duties in matters relating to maintaining such safeguards and
8 protections; provided, however that nothing in this subdivision shall be
9 deemed to authorize such officer to carry, possess, repair or dispose of
10 a firearm unless the appropriate license therefor has been issued pursu-
11 ant to section 400.00 of the penal law.
12 &#167; 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
