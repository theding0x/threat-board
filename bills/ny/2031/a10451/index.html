NY · 2023-2024 GENERAL ASSEMBLY · INTRODUCED (05-24)

NY A10451 — Enacts "Francesco's law" which establishes that a person is guilty of failure to safely store rifles, shotguns, and firearms in the presence of a minor in the second degree if such improper storage leads to an individual under the age of 18 injuring themselves or another and in the first degree if such storage leads to an individual under the age of 18 killing themselves or another.

ANTI SPONSORS: 1 D

WHY IT WAS FLAGGED

This bill creates new criminal penalties for unsafe firearm storage, directly tightening restrictions on gun owners and adding gun control measures requiring secure storage when minors are present.
