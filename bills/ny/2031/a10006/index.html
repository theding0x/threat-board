NY · 2023-2024 GENERAL ASSEMBLY · INTRODUCED (05-01)

NY A10006 — Provides for tenant responses to applications for a major capital improvement rent increase; allows tenants to respond within one hundred twenty days from the date of mailing of a notice of a proceeding; requires the state division of housing and community renewal to provide any responding tenant with the reasons for the division's approval or denial of such application.

PRO SPONSORS: 2 D
INTROCMTEFLOORPASSEDLAW
WHY IT WAS FLAGGED

Bill strengthens tenant protections by extending response periods (120 days for major capital improvements, 60 days for temporary increases) and requiring the division to provide reasoned decisions, limiting landlords' unilateral rent increase authority and protecting tenant affordability.
