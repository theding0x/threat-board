NY · 2023-2024 GENERAL ASSEMBLY · INTRODUCED (04-26)

NY A09948 — Adds a person with a disability who has their primary residence in a special needs trust, or a property owner who has a tenant with a disability whose lease provides them with a life interest in the property as long as the tenant remains in residence as eligible for a real property tax exemption pursuant to section 459-c of the real property tax law.

PRO UNDER REVIEW SPONSORS: 4 D · 3 R
INTROCMTEFLOORPASSEDLAW
WHY IT WAS FLAGGED

Bill expands housing eligibility protections for people with disabilities through special needs trusts and life-interest tenant leases, indirectly supporting affordable housing access for vulnerable populations and incentivizing landlords to house disabled tenants through tax exemptions.
