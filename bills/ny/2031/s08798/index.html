AN ACT to amend the highway law, in relation to designating a portion of
the state highway system as the "Sergeant James S. Hayes Memorial
Highway"
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. The highway law is amended by adding a new section 344-p to
2 read as follows:
3 &#167; 344-p. Portion of the state highway system to be designated as the
4 "Sergeant James S. Hayes Memorial Highway". All that portion of the
5 state highway system constituting state route 14 beginning at the inter-
6 section of state route 14 and Plum Point Road in the town of Milo, coun-
7 ty of Yates, continuing in a generally northerly direction, and termi-
8 nating at the intersection of state route 14 and state route 54, also
9 known as Main Street, in the village of Dresden, county of Yates, shall
10 be designated and known as the "Sergeant James S. Hayes Memorial High-
11 way".
12 &#167; 2. The commissioner of transportation shall provide for the instal-
13 lation and maintenance of adequate signing of the state highway system
14 as designated pursuant to section one of this act. However, to avoid
15 confusion and to limit any possible disruption of commerce, the desig-
16 nation called for pursuant to section one of this act shall be one of
17 ceremonial nature and the official name of such highway shall not be
18 changed as a result of this act.
19 &#167; 3. This act shall take effect immediately.
