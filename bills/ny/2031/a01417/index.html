NY A01417 — Relates to the sale, lease or other disposition of property of the city, New York city department of education, New York city educational construction fund, New York city school construction authority and New York city housing authority; requires all disposition to be subject to New York city's uniform land use review procedure; requires prior notification and community consultation.

NEUTRAL UNDER REVIEW SPONSORS: 1 D
INTROCMTEFLOORPASSEDLAW
WHY IT WAS FLAGGED

Bill requires community consultation and ULURP review for housing authority and education department property dispositions, which could indirectly influence affordable housing outcomes, but does not directly mandate or reduce affordable housing supply or tenant protections.
