AN ACT to amend the penal law, in relation to establishing the crime of
feticide
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. The penal law is amended by adding a new section 125.24 to
2 read as follows:
3 &#167; 125.24 Feticide.
4 A person is guilty of feticide when with intent to cause the death of
5 an unborn child at any stage of gestation, he or she causes the termi-
6 nation of a woman's pregnancy resulting in the death of such unborn
7 child at any stage of gestation, except when such termination occurs in
8 accordance with section twenty-five hundred ninety-nine-bb of the public
9 health law.
10 Feticide is a class A felony.
11 &#167; 2. This act shall take effect on the ninetieth day after it shall
12 have become a law.
