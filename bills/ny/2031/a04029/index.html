NY A04029 — Establishes liaison officers, regional contacts and campus contacts to assist college students experiencing homelessness to find housing; prioritizes access to housing; waives certain fees for students experiencing homelessness; establishes a pilot program to increase enrollment, attendance and success in college for students experiencing homelessness.

WHY IT WAS FLAGGED

Bill directly increases housing access and protections for homeless college students through priority housing allocation, housing fee waivers, housing navigation services, and a pilot program designating funding for housing resources, which enhances affordable housing supply and tenant protections for this vulnerable population.
