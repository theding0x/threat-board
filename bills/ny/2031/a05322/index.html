NY · 2023-2024 GENERAL ASSEMBLY · INTRODUCED (03-07)

NY A05322 — Enacts the "packaging reduction and recycling infrastructure act" to require companies selling, offering for sale, or distributing packaging materials and products to register with a packaging reduction organization to develop a packaging reduction and recycling plan.

PRO UNDER REVIEW SPONSORS: 75 D · 1 R
INTROCMTEFLOORPASSEDLAW
WHY IT WAS FLAGGED

The bill establishes a packaging reduction program with a funding mechanism (section 27-3413) that would finance collection infrastructure, convenience improvements, and recycling systems through producer fees, effectively creating public-works infrastructure funding for waste management and recycling collection systems.
