NY · 2023-2024 GENERAL ASSEMBLY · INTRODUCED (03-10)

NY S05659 — Provides for tenant responses to applications for a major capital improvement rent increase; allows tenants to respond within one hundred twenty days from the date of mailing of a notice of a proceeding; requires the state division of housing and community renewal to provide any responding tenant with the reasons for the division's approval or denial of such application.

WHY IT WAS FLAGGED

Bill strengthens tenant protections in rent-controlled housing by providing tenants 120 days to respond to major capital improvement rent increases and requiring disclosure of approval/denial reasons, directly supporting tenant rights in affordable housing.
