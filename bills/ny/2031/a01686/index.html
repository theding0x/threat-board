AN ACT to amend the private housing finance law, in relation to requir-
ing the division of housing and community renewal to provide an annual
report on the New York access to home programs
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. The private housing finance law is amended by adding a new
2 section 1203 to read as follows:
3 &#167; 1203. Report. 1. The division of housing and community renewal shall
4 issue an annual report on the access to home programs regarding certain
5 data and program progress for the previous year. Such report shall
6 include, but not be limited to:
7 (a) the number of contracts the corporation entered into with eligible
8 applicants and the amount of funding both awarded and expended by
9 successful applicants;
10 (b) the number of projects each eligible recipient completed;
11 (c) the types of projects completed by each eligible recipient,
12 including the average cost of each type of project;
13 (d) the geographic diversity of the beneficiaries of each completed
14 project;
15 (e) the demographic information of the beneficiaries of each completed
16 project, including differentiating between renters and homeowners;
17 (f) the unmet demand for access to home programs who applied for fund-
18 ing through an access to home programs;
19 (g) how many persons with eligible properties were denied and the
20 demographic information regarding such denied individuals; and
21 (h) the average timeline for completion of each project.
