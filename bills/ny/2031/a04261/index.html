NY A04261 — Establishes a ten business day waiting period from contact of the New York State National Instant Background Check System (NYS NICS) before a firearm, shotgun or rifle may be delivered to a person.

WHY IT WAS FLAGGED

Bill reduces the waiting period from 30 calendar days to 10 business days before firearm delivery, which tightens firearm restrictions by adding a mandatory waiting period requirement—a gun control measure.
