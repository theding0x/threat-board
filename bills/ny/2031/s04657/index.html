NY · 2023-2024 GENERAL ASSEMBLY · INTRODUCED (02-13)

NY S04657 — Establishes the class A-I felony of criminal use of a firearm for possessing a deadly weapon or displaying what appears to be or is a rifle, pistol, revolver, shotgun, machine gun or other firearm during the commission of a class A or violent felony; repeals the offenses of criminal use of a firearm in the first and second degrees.

ANTI SPONSORS: 1 R
INTROCMTEFLOORPASSEDLAW
WHY IT WAS FLAGGED

This bill consolidates existing firearm criminal offenses into a new Class A-I felony with enhanced penalties, tightening restrictions on firearm use during violent crimes and maintaining or strengthening gun control measures rather than loosening them.
