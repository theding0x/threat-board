NY S01657 — Provides that possession and use of a firearm in certain locations under the supervision, guidance and instruction of a qualified firearms instructor shall not be a crime.

WHY IT WAS FLAGGED

Bill expands gun rights by broadening the definition of 'qualified firearms instructor' to include instructors certified by any division of criminal justice services-recognized organization, allowing more people to supervise firearms use at ranges and lowering barriers to pre-license pistol possession and training.
