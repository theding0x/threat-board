NY A00245 — Prohibits landlords from including incorrect information relating to rent decontrol in certain leases and renewals thereof; imposes a violation punishable by a fine of $1000 for a violation by a landlord; requires the standardization of certain notices pertaining to units subject to the Affordable New York Housing Program.

WHY IT WAS FLAGGED

Bill protects rent-stabilized and rent-regulated tenants by requiring accurate lease disclosures about rent decontrol dates and standardizing tenant protections under the 421-a affordable housing program, preventing landlord deception that could harm tenant housing stability.
