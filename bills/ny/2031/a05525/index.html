AN ACT to amend the private housing finance law, in relation to requir-
ing five percent of affordable housing units to be offered to veterans
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. The private housing finance law is amended by adding a new
2 section 602-a to read as follows:
3 &#167; 602-a. Housing for veterans. All companies, corporations, and
4 programs subject to the provisions of this chapter shall offer, at a
5 minimum, five percent of affordable housing units built by such compa-
6 nies corporations and programs to veterans, as such term is defined
7 pursuant to section eighty-five of the civil service law.
8 &#167; 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
