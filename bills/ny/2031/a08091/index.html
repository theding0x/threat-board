NY · 2023-2024 GENERAL ASSEMBLY · INTRODUCED (09-29)

NY A08091 — Defines humanitarian emergency response and relief centers as shelters that provide assistance to arriving asylees or that provide shared sleeping and bathroom facilities for more than 96 hours and includes such centers within the definition of homeless project.

PRO UNDER REVIEW SPONSORS: 2 D
INTROCMTEFLOORPASSEDLAW
WHY IT WAS FLAGGED

Bill expands the definition of 'homeless project' to include humanitarian emergency response and relief centers, which facilitates shelter provision for asylees and vulnerable populations, thereby increasing housing availability for persons experiencing homelessness or housing insecurity.

3 6. "Humanitarian emergency response and relief center" shall mean a
