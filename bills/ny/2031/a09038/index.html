AN ACT relating to funding for highway construction and improvements in
western New York
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. The sum of two billion dollars ($2,000,000,000), or so much
2 thereof as may be necessary, is hereby appropriated to the highway and
3 bridge capital account of the dedicated highway and bridge trust fund,
4 out of any moneys appropriated to the metropolitan transportation
5 authority in the 2024 state fiscal plan, and made immediately available
6 for construction, reconstruction, enhancement and improvement of state,
7 county, town, city, and village roads, highways, parkways, and bridges
8 thereon in New York state. Such moneys shall be payable on the audit and
9 warrant of the comptroller on vouchers certified or approved by the
10 commissioner of transportation in the manner prescribed by law.
11 &#167; 2. This act shall take effect April 1, 2024.
