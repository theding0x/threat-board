The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-

1 Section 1. Subdivision 2 of section 3006-a of the education law, as

3 2015, is amended to read as follows:
4 2. a. During each five-year registration period beginning on or after

15 effectiveness requirements.
16 b. (i) Such continuing teacher and leader education required pursuant

18 hours of continuing education courses, programs and activities related
19 to immigrant focused trauma-informed practices to support students who
20 are dealing with adverse childhood or adolescent experiences as a result
21 of immigration status. For purposes of this paragraph, "immigrant
