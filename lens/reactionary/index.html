Reactionary

A bill is REACTIONARY (Marxist sense) when it works to restore, protect, or return to an older, more oppressive order by rolling back the rights or gains of the working class. The working class includes LGBTQ+ people, immigrants, women, tenants, and workers of every kind — their rights are working-class rights. Bills that restrict abortion access, strip LGBTQ+ protections, target immigrants, or gut tenant/labor protections are attacks on the working class and are reactionary. Economic and social reaction are one axis, not two: do not treat social-rights rollbacks as separate from or lesser than economic ones. A bill that merely maintains the status quo, or that advances working-class rights, is NOT reactionary.
